Mergers & Acquisitions (M&A) Advisory Services

Buy-side and sell-side support from mandate to post-close: market mapping, indicative valuation, diligence PMO, SPA/APA coordination with counsel, and Cyprus-based execution for cross-border transactions.

What We Do

  • Mandate scoping, workplan, and critical path for buy-side/sell-side or carve-outs.
  • Target search, longlist/shortlist, outreach tracking, and confidentiality workflows.
  • Indicative valuation (comps/precedents/simple DCF) and decision memos.
  • Teaser/IM and management presentation drafting, QC, and version control.
  • Virtual data room (VDR) build: structure, permissions, indexing, and Q&A cadence.
  • Diligence PMO: financial/tax/legal/tech workstream coordination with advisors.
  • Term sheet & SPA/APA issues list management and redline loop support.
  • Closing mechanics, funds flow, filings, and day-1/post-close integration planning.

Process

  1. Scope & Readiness: Define objectives, constraints, deal perimeter, and data hygiene. Set roles, comms channels, and reporting cadence.
  2. Market & Valuation: Map targets/buyers, create longlist/shortlist, initiate outreach. Prepare indicative valuation & decision notes.
  3. Diligence PMO: Stand-up VDR, Q&A tracker, and standing agendas. Coordinate advisors, issue logs, and weekly status.
  4. Docs & Approvals: Term sheet to SPA/APA: manage issues list with counsel, align conditions precedent, and stakeholder approvals.
  5. Closing & Funds Flow: Execute signing/closing checklists, filings, and settlements. Compile closing binder.
  6. Integration Day-1: Mobilise Day-1 plan, owner comms, access/controls, and first-90-days KPIs.

FAQ

Information here is general and not legal, tax, or investment advice. We coordinate with licensed professionals where required.

Do you replace legal counsel or investment banks?
No. We act as a corporate/M&A PMO and execution partner. Counsel drafts legal instruments (term sheet, SPA/APA), and any regulated placement or investment services are handled by licensed firms.
What deal sizes and sectors do you support?
Primarily SME and mid-market transactions (≈€1m–€50m enterprise value), across Cyprus and CEE, with cross-border coordination. We adapt to earlier or larger stages if scope is well-defined.
What is a typical timeline for an SME deal involving Cyprus?
Indicative: 8–16 weeks from mandate to signing, plus 2–6 weeks to close depending on approvals and conditions precedent. Actual timing depends on diligence complexity and third-party responsiveness.
How do you protect confidentiality and data?
Mutual NDAs, segregated VDR with role-based permissions, access logs, and structured Q&A. Sensitive documents are shared only via the VDR, not email.
How are fees structured?
Fixed-fee modules or monthly retainer for PMO, with optional success components via separate agreement. Disbursements (filings, notarisation, translations) are billed at cost.
