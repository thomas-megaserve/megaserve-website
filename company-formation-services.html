Who It’s For

  • Entrepreneurs and SMEs seeking an EU operating vehicle in Cyprus
  • Groups relocating functions to optimise operations and compliance footprint
  • Founders needing bank-ready documentation and post-incorporation support

What You Get (Deliverables)

  • Company name clearance and constitutional documents (MoA/AoA)
  • Registrar filings (HE forms) and full certificate set (Incorporation, Directors/Secretary, Shareholders, Registered Office)
  • Beneficial ownership register filing (as applicable)
  • Post-incorporation pack and bank/KYB documentation preparation (details)
  • Engagement handover to Corporate Secretarial and Registered Address (if required)
  • Related add-ons (scoped separately): TIC/TIN, VAT, apostille/legalisation via Administrative Services
• Cyprus-first focus
• Banking introductions & onboarding support*
• Checklists to reduce rework

*Bank account approval is always at the bank’s discretion; we pre-triage and support onboarding but do not guarantee outcomes.

Process & Typical Timeline

  1. Discovery & KYC pre-screen — scope, sector/geo review, initial document list (~1–2 business days)
  2. Name approval & drafting — MoA/AoA and HE forms prepared (typically ~1–3 business days)
  3. Registrar filing — incorporation and certificate issuance (commonly ~3–7 business days, dependent on registrar workload)
  4. Post-incorporation — company pack delivered; bank/KYB pack readied (typically ~1–3 business days)
  5. Optional handover — to Tax & VAT team for TIC/TIN, VAT, EORI (timelines vary by authority)

Overall guide: many standard incorporations complete in ~5–10 business days, subject to due diligence and authority/registrar timing.

Requirements (Client Checklist)

  • Certified passports/IDs for UBOs, directors, and shareholders
  • Recent proof of address (≤ 3 months)
  • UBO/shareholding chart
  • Business activity summary (goods/services, countries, expected volumes)
  • Source-of-funds/source-of-wealth narrative
  • Draft contracts or key counterparties (if available)
  • Power of Attorney (if required for signing)

Risk & Mitigations

  • Banking acceptance: no guarantees; we match to suitable institutions and prepare KYB to reduce friction.
  • EDD sectors/geographies: additional information and timing may apply; we flag this early in scoping.
  • Document quality: certified IDs/addresses must meet standards; we provide formats and samples to avoid resubmissions.
  • Name restrictions: we pre-check for sensitive words and conflicts to avoid registrar rejections.

FAQs

Do I need to visit Cyprus to incorporate?

No. Incorporation can typically be completed remotely. Banks may require in-person verification depending on the institution and risk profile.

How long does incorporation take?

Standard cases often complete in ~5–10 business days from final KYC and name approval. Timings depend on registrar workload and document readiness.

Can you guarantee a bank account?

No. We provide introductions and onboarding support, but approval rests solely with the bank. We pre-triage your profile to avoid mismatches.

Is a local director or secretary required?

A corporate secretary and registered office are required. A resident director is not strictly mandated, but substance expectations may apply depending on your activities. We can arrange these via our Corporate Secretarial and Directorship services.

Can you handle TIC/TIN and VAT registrations?

Yes—coordinated through our Tax & VAT Administration pages post-incorporation.

Do you support redomiciliation or restructuring?

Yes. See our related pages for company structuring/re-structuring and migrations, or request a proposal and we’ll scope it alongside your formation.
