Company Formation Services

Full-cycle incorporation in Cyprus: advisory, documentation, registrar filings, and bank coordination—delivered by a multilingual, experienced team.

What’s Included

  • Jurisdiction & structure advisory tailored to commercial goals
  • Name checks, drafting MoA/AoA, and registrar submissions
  • UBO, director, and shareholder documentation guidance
  • Registered address & secretarial arrangements (as needed)
  • Bank/KYB preparation and introductions

Typical Timeline

Standard incorporations complete in 5–10 business days, subject to due diligence and registrar workload.

Process

  1. Scoping call and KYC/KYB checks
  2. Documentation & drafting
  3. Registrar filings & issuance of corporate docs
  4. Banking introductions and account opening support
