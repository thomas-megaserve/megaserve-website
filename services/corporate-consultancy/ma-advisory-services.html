Who It’s For

  • Buyers and sellers who want a disciplined PMO, clean data room structure, and predictable diligence cadence
  • Cross-border transactions involving Cyprus entities, assets, or operating footprints
  • SME and mid-market deals where execution discipline matters more than “big bank” theatre

What You Get (Deliverables)

  • Mandate brief and critical path workplan
  • Target/bidder mapping workbook (longlist/shortlist & outreach log)
  • Indicative valuation memo (assumptions + sensitivities)
  • VDR index, permissions register, and structured Q&A tracker
  • Issues list and decision log coordinated with counsel
  • Closing checklist and closing binder (signatures, filings, funds flow)
  • Day-1 integration plan and first-90-days actions/KPIs

Process & Typical Timeline

  1. Scope & Readiness: define objectives, perimeter, roles, and reporting cadence; clean up data hygiene.
  2. Market & Valuation: map targets/buyers, outreach tracking, and prepare an indicative valuation note.
  3. Diligence PMO: stand up the VDR, Q&A tracker, and issue logs; coordinate advisors and weekly status.
  4. Docs & Approvals: manage issues list with counsel, conditions precedent, and stakeholder approvals.
  5. Closing & Funds Flow: execute signing/closing checklists, filings, and settlement timetable; compile closing binder.
  6. Integration Day-1: mobilise access/controls, comms, and first-90-day KPI tracking.

Case Study

Asset Purchase with Property/Lease Transition (Anonymised)

Client: Buyer acquiring operating assets and assuming a new property/lease footprint (anonymised)

The client pursued an asset purchase where the transaction scope included operating assets and a property/lease transition. The key execution risk was controlling diligence, keeping documentation clean, aligning counsel on the asset purchase agreement issues list, and coordinating practical steps for premises and day-1 operations.

MegaServe coordinated the diligence PMO, VDR structure and Q&A cadence, compiled an issues tracker for counsel (assets, assignment/novation points, closing deliverables), and maintained a closing checklist covering payments, handover documents, and post-close access/controls. Property/lease items were tracked as a defined workstream to avoid last-minute gaps.

Result: a controlled closing process with documented handover steps and a clean closing binder suitable for banks, auditors, and counterparties.

FAQs

Do you replace legal counsel or investment banks?

No. We act as the deal PMO and execution partner. Counsel drafts legal instruments, and any regulated investment services are handled by licensed firms.

What deal sizes do you support?

Primarily SME and mid-market transactions, with cross-border coordination where Cyprus entities or assets are involved. Scope and readiness drive feasibility.

How do you protect confidentiality and data?

Mutual NDAs, segregated VDR with role-based permissions, access logs, and structured Q&A. Sensitive documents are shared only via the VDR, not email.

How are fees structured?

Fixed-fee modules or monthly retainer for PMO/execution, with optional success components via separate agreement. Disbursements (filings, notarisation, translations) billed at cost.
