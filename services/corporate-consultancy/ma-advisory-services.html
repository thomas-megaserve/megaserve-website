Who It’s For

  • Owner-managed businesses preparing for a sale or partial exit
  • Buyers acquiring assets or operations in Cyprus, including lease assignments
  • Groups needing disciplined VDR/Q&A control and adviser coordination

What You Get (Deliverables)

  • Mandate brief & critical path workplan
  • VDR build, permissions register, and Q&A tracker
  • Issues list and decision log across workstreams
  • Closing checklist, filings tracker, and closing binder pack
  • Post-close day-1 and first-90-days integration plan (where requested)

Process & Typical Timeline

  1. Scope & Readiness: objectives, constraints, deal perimeter, and data hygiene.
  2. Market & Valuation: mapping, outreach tracking, indicative valuation notes.
  3. Diligence PMO: VDR + Q&A workflow; adviser coordination; weekly status.
  4. Docs & Approvals: issues list management with counsel; CPs and approvals.
  5. Closing: signing/closing checklist, filings, settlements, closing binder.
  6. Integration: day-1 controls, comms, access, and first-90-days actions.

Case Study

Asset Purchase with Property Lease Assignment (Anonymised)

Transaction: Asset purchase (non-share deal) with lease assignment for operating premises

The buyer acquired a set of operating assets and ongoing contracts, while needing continuity of the business location through a lease assignment and landlord consent. The transaction required tight sequencing across diligence, documentation, and completion steps so that operations could transition without disruption.

MegaServe coordinated the diligence workflow and VDR/Q&A process, maintained an issues list across workstreams, and supported completion mechanics including the lease assignment steps and closing checklist. Documentation was kept controlled and indexed for a clean closing binder and post-close handover.

Result: asset transfer and lease transition completed with controlled documentation and a clear handover pack.

FAQs

Informational only — not legal, tax, or investment advice. Legal instruments are drafted by licensed counsel.

Do you replace legal counsel or investment banks?

No. We act as an execution and coordination function (PMO). Counsel drafts legal instruments (term sheet, SPA/APA). Any regulated investment services are handled by licensed firms.

Do you support asset purchases as well as share deals?

Yes. We coordinate both, including asset schedules, assumed liabilities tracking, and practical items like lease assignment and handover sequencing.

How do you protect confidentiality and data?

Mutual NDAs, segregated VDR with role-based permissions and access logs, and structured Q&A. Sensitive documents are shared only via the VDR.

What is a typical timeline?

Indicative: 8–16 weeks from mandate to signing, plus 2–6 weeks to close depending on approvals and conditions precedent. Actual timing depends on diligence complexity and third-party responsiveness.

How are fees structured?

Fixed-fee modules or monthly retainer for PMO, with optional success components via separate agreement. Disbursements (filings, notarisation, translations) are billed at cost.
