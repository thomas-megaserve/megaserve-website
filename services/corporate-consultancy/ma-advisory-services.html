Process

  1. Scope & Readiness: Define objectives, constraints, deal perimeter, and data hygiene. Set roles, comms channels, and reporting cadence.
  2. Market & Valuation: Map targets/buyers, create longlist/shortlist, initiate outreach. Prepare indicative valuation & decision notes.
  3. Diligence PMO: Stand up VDR, Q&A tracker, and standing agendas. Coordinate advisors, issue logs, and weekly status.
  4. Docs & Approvals: From term sheet to SPA/APA: manage issues list with counsel, align CPs, and stakeholder approvals.
  5. Closing & Funds Flow: Execute signing/closing checklists, filings, and settlements. Compile closing binder.
  6. Integration Day-1: Mobilise Day-1 plan, owner comms, access/controls, and first-90-days KPIs.

FAQs

Do you replace legal counsel or investment banks?

No. We act as a corporate/M&A PMO and execution partner. Counsel drafts legal instruments (term sheet, SPA/APA), and any regulated placement or investment services are handled by licensed firms.

What deal sizes and sectors do you support?

Primarily SME and mid-market transactions (approximately €1m–€50m enterprise value), across Cyprus and CEE, with cross-border coordination. We adapt to earlier or larger stages if scope is well-defined.

What is a typical timeline for an SME deal involving Cyprus?

Indicative: 8–16 weeks from mandate to signing, plus 2–6 weeks to close depending on approvals and conditions precedent. Actual timing depends on diligence complexity and third-party responsiveness.

How do you protect confidentiality and data?

Mutual NDAs, segregated data room with role-based permissions, access logs, and structured Q&A. Sensitive documents are shared only via the data room, not email.

How are fees structured?

Fixed-fee modules or monthly retainer for execution coordination, with optional success components via separate agreement. Disbursements (filings, notarisation, translations) are billed at cost.
