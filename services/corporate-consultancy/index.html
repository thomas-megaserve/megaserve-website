Overview

Consultancy is only useful if it produces decisions and execution—not slides. We structure work into clear deliverables, written conclusions, and a practical plan that can be executed and audited.

Who It’s For

  • Owners and boards needing decision support for acquisitions, restructurings, or strategic pivots
  • Teams requiring integration planning and “execution glue” across functions and vendors
  • Groups needing a project manager to deliver cross-party outcomes to a defined timeline

How We Work

  1. Scope: define the decision to be made and what “done” means (outputs, owners, deadlines).
  2. Inputs: collect documents and facts into a controlled folder structure.
  3. Analysis: produce a written memo, risk map, and options with clear trade-offs.
  4. Plan: convert the chosen option into a delivery plan (workstreams, milestones, dependencies).
  5. Execution: coordinate stakeholders and track delivery with status reporting.

FAQs

Do you replace lawyers, auditors, or accountants?

No. We coordinate and structure the work, but regulated advice remains with licensed professionals. We keep the process organised and decision-ready.

Do you provide a written output?

Yes. Where applicable, we deliver a written memo, decision options, and an execution plan—not just meetings.

Can you manage multi-party projects?

Yes. Project management exists to coordinate vendors, advisors, and stakeholders with clear milestones and reporting.

How fast can you start?

Typically within a few business days, depending on scope and document readiness. Email your objective and timeline for a quick intake.
