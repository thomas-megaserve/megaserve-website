Who It’s For

  • Owners and executives who need predictable delivery and clear decisions
  • Cross-functional projects with vendors, dependencies, and deadlines
  • Regulatory or evidence-heavy projects where documentation matters

What You Get

  • Project charter (scope, objectives, success criteria, constraints)
  • Plan and dependency map with owners and target dates
  • RAID register (risks, assumptions, issues, decisions) with owners and due dates
  • Weekly status pack (progress, blockers, decisions needed)
  • Cutover/runbook and go/no-go criteria (if there is a launch)
  • Post-implementation review and handover pack

Process

  1. Charter: confirm scope, stakeholders, and KPIs. Set roles and reporting.
  2. Plan: build the schedule, dependencies, and acceptance criteria. Start the RAID log.
  3. Execute: run the cadence, manage vendors, and track actions to closure.
  4. Control changes: log change requests with impact notes and approvals.
  5. Cutover: prepare the runbook, rehearsal steps, and Day-1 support (if needed).
  6. Close: handover pack, lessons learned, and next-step actions.

Workstream Leads Own deliverables and report blockers and risks. Weekly / Workstream minutes, actions and blockers.

Case Study

Real Estate Acquisition + Completion Coordination + Ongoing Property Management (Anonymised)

Client: Overseas buyer acquiring a Cyprus residential property (anonymised)

The client needed a controlled sequence across parties (seller/developer, lawyers, bank/payment steps, and local authorities). The main risk was delay due to missing documents or unclear ownership of actions.

MegaServe used a single plan with responsibilities, deadlines, and an actions/issues log. We coordinated document checks, signatures, payment sequencing, completion steps, and post-completion setup. FAQs

Do you replace internal project managers or consultants?

No. We run the PMO and governance, integrate with your team, and coordinate vendors. Specialist design/build tasks remain with internal teams or appointed firms.

What tools do you use for scheduling and tracking?

We adapt to your toolset and we can provide simple templates for plan, RAID, actions, and decisions.

Can you help with projects involving regulators, banks, or authorities?

Yes. We track timelines and evidence lists and keep liaison notes. Regulatory positions and submissions remain with your licensed advisors.

How do you handle change requests without derailing timelines?

We log changes with scope/cost/time impact, get owner approval, and schedule changes against priorities and buffers.

Do you guarantee deadlines?

No. Delivery depends on third parties and data quality. We set realistic gates, flag risks early, and keep decisions documented.
