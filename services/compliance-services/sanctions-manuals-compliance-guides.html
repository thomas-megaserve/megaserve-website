Sanctions Manuals & Compliance Guides

Implement sanctions controls that teams can execute daily—clear regime coverage, list-update routines, 50% ownership rules, calibrated screening, and defensible escalation notes.

What We Do

  • Regime mapping: UN, EU, UK, OFAC, and local regimes relevant to your footprint.
  • List management SOPs: update sources, backfills, release handling, and proofs.
  • Name-matching calibration: thresholds, fuzzy/alias rules, transliteration (non-Latin).
  • Ownership & control rules: 50% aggregation, acting-on-behalf, and control indicators.
  • Sectoral/territorial measures and circumvention red flags incorporated into flows.
  • Screening scope: customers, UBOs, signatories, counterparties, vessels/entities.
  • Escalation & licensing: decision trees, counsel/bank coordination, and records.
  • Registers & MI: hits, true positives, exceptions, change log, and board/MLRO reporting.

Process

  1. Scope & Baseline: Confirm regimes, jurisdictions, products, risk appetite, and current tooling.
  2. Map Obligations: Translate regime requirements into screening, ownership, and monitoring controls.
  3. Configure & Calibrate: Tune lists, match thresholds, aliases/transliteration, and user roles.
  4. Train & Roll Out: Playbooks, desk guides, and role-based training with attestations.
  5. Operate & Report: Triage hits, escalate potential TPs, maintain registers and MI.
  6. Review & Update: Periodic effectiveness testing, change log, and audit/exam support.

FAQ

Informational only—this is not legal or regulatory advice. Final positions rest with your Compliance/MLRO, counsel, and relevant authorities/banks.

Which regimes do you cover?
UN, EU, UK, OFAC (US), and any local/sectoral regimes relevant to your footprint—documented in the mapping note with refresh cadence.
Do you provide legal advice or licensing services?
No. We document and operate processes and evidence. Legal interpretations and licensing/derogation requests are handled by your counsel and authorities.
How quickly are list updates deployed?
Same day for major regimes where tools allow automated sync; manual proofs and impact notes are filed when automation isn’t available.
Can you guarantee zero sanctions exposure?
No. We implement risk-based controls, QA, and documentation to reduce error and demonstrate diligence, but cannot eliminate risk.
How do you handle the “50% rule” and complex ownership?
We apply aggregation and control tests per policy, capture rationale, and escalate edge cases with supporting evidence for MLRO/counsel review.
