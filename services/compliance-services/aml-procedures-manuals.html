Anti-Money Laundering (AML) Procedures & Manuals

Build or refresh an AML framework that teams actually use—risk assessment, procedures, SAR playbooks, training, and MI—documented and evidence-ready for banks, auditors, and regulators.

What We Do

  • Business-wide ML/TF Risk Assessment (BRA) methodology and heatmap.
  • CDD/EDD/SDD procedures with clear evidence thresholds and templates.
  • Ownership & control (UBO) mapping rules and SoW/SoF documentation.
  • Screening linkages (sanctions/PEP/adverse media) and escalation routes.
  • Monitoring & trigger events: what to review, when, and how to record.
  • SAR/STR workflow: suspicion tests, drafting notes, approvals, and filing steps.
  • Training packs by role with attestations register and pass/fail logs.
  • Record retention schedule, privacy notices, and MI for board/MLRO reports.

Process

  1. Baseline & Risk Appetite: Review current policies, products, jurisdictions, and counterpart/bank expectations.
  2. Design: Draft BRA approach, CDD/EDD/SDD procedures, screening linkage, and SAR workflow with approvals.
  3. Build & Templates: Produce manuals, checklists, playbooks, registers, and MI packs; align retention/privacy.
  4. Pilot & Train: Run sample files, tune thresholds, deliver role-based training, collect attestations.
  5. Publish & Implement: Versioned approvals, change log, go-live notes, and calendar of reviews.
  6. Review & Improve: Quarterly/annual updates, exception/assumptions register, and audit support.

FAQ

Informational only—this is not legal or regulatory advice. Final positions rest with your Compliance/MLRO and advisors.

Are the AML manuals templates or fully tailored?
Tailored. We start from proven structures and align to your risk appetite, products, jurisdictions, and counterpart expectations.
Do you act as MLRO?
No. We support your MLRO and team with drafting, training, and evidence packs. Designated roles and final decisions remain with your organisation.
Can you guarantee regulator or bank approval?
No guarantees. We align to policy and guidance, document decisions, and prepare audit-ready files to reduce friction during reviews.
How often should the AML policy and BRA be reviewed?
At least annually or on material change (products, jurisdictions, thresholds). We provide a review calendar and change log.
Can you integrate procedures with screening and onboarding?
Yes. We embed screening and KYC steps, link to onboarding files, and align evidence/approvals across functions.
