Who It’s For

  • Law firms, corporate service providers, and regulated businesses that need an AML manual that is actually executable by staff
  • Teams preparing for bank onboarding, audits, regulator interactions, or client due diligence reviews
  • Organisations that want consistent CDD/EDD standards, documented escalation routes, and evidence-ready records

What You Get (Deliverables)

  • AML framework outline and Business Risk Assessment (BRA) structure (methodology + heatmap template)
  • CDD/EDD/SDD procedures manual with checklists and evidence thresholds
  • UBO + SoW/SoF documentation templates and escalation notes
  • SAR/STR playbook, internal workflow templates, and registers (as applicable)
  • Training deck, quiz, attendance/attestation logs, and review calendar
  • Audit-ready repository structure and version/change log discipline

Process & Typical Timeline

  1. Baseline & risk appetite: review current policies, products/services, jurisdictions, and counterpart expectations.
  2. Design: draft BRA approach, CDD/EDD/SDD procedures, screening linkage, and escalation routes.
  3. Build: manuals, checklists, playbooks, registers, and MI packs; align retention/privacy and access controls.
  4. Pilot & train: run sample files, tune thresholds, deliver role-based training, collect attestations.
  5. Publish: versioned approvals, change log, go-live notes, and calendar of reviews.

FAQs

Are the AML manuals templates or fully tailored?

Tailored. We start from proven structures and align to your risk appetite, products, jurisdictions, and counterpart expectations.

Do you act as MLRO?

No. We support your MLRO and team with drafting, training, and evidence packs. Designated roles and final decisions remain with your organisation.

Can you guarantee regulator or bank approval?

No guarantees. We align to policy and guidance, document decisions, and prepare audit-ready files to reduce friction during reviews.

How often should the AML policy and BRA be reviewed?

At least annually or on material change (products, jurisdictions, thresholds). We provide a review calendar and change log.

Can you integrate procedures with screening and onboarding?

Yes. We embed screening and KYC steps, link to onboarding files, and align evidence/approvals across functions.
