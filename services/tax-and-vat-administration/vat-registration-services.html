VAT Registration Services

Get VAT-ready in Cyprus with a clean application, correct evidencing, and a post-registration setup that banks, auditors, and counterparties recognise.

What We Handle

  • Eligibility check: thresholds, fixed establishment, place-of-supply and activity profile
  • Registration type selection (domestic, non-established, group, special schemes where applicable)
  • Application pack preparation and authority liaison
  • EORI (if needed for imports) and VIES (intra-EU) setup notes
  • e-Services activation and handover to your tax advisor
  • Post-registration calendar: return periods, VIES/Intrastat (if thresholds apply), reminders

When Registration Is Typically Required

  • Local supplies of goods/services in Cyprus reaching or expected to reach statutory thresholds
  • Intra-EU acquisitions or supplies and distance-selling scenarios
  • Importing goods into the EU via Cyprus or holding stock locally
  • Voluntary registration to recover input VAT (subject to rules)

We avoid fixed thresholds in writing here—your tax advisor confirms exact numeric tests and effective dates.

Registration Options (At a Glance)

Option Typical Use Strengths Considerations
Domestic VAT Number Cyprus-based trading with local inputs/outputs Standard compliance baseline; bank/auditor familiarity Regular returns; VIES/Intrastat if applicable
Non-Established Registration No fixed establishment, but taxable supplies in Cyprus Enables local charging/recovery where allowed Representation or local address may be needed
VAT Group (where eligible) Related entities seeking internal supply simplification Simplifies filings; potential cash-flow benefits Eligibility and governance requirements apply
OSS / IOSS (EU Schemes) Cross-border B2C supplies of services/goods Single return for multiple EU states (scope-dependent) Scheme scope is specific—coordinate with tax advisor

We’ll outline viable paths and coordinate with your advisors to confirm the optimal route.

Process & Typical Timeline

  1. Scoping & Pre-check: activity profile, supply chains, and documents list.
  2. Drafting & Evidence: forms, supporting contracts/invoices, and certification instructions.
  3. Submission & Clarifications: lodge application; respond to queries; arrange e-Services.
  4. Issuance & Handover: VAT number/certificate provided; bank/auditor confirmations sent.
  5. Stabilise: calendar setup, VIES/Intrastat notes, user access guide, and first-return checklist.

FAQ

How long does VAT registration take?
Timing depends on authority workload and file complexity. We focus on clean, complete submissions to avoid rework.
Can we register voluntarily?
Often yes—subject to rules and your facts. We coordinate with your tax advisor to confirm eligibility and implications.
Do we need OSS/IOSS as well?
Only for specific cross-border B2C scenarios. We’ll flag if your activity suggests exploring these schemes.
Will you notify our bank and auditor?
Yes—on request we issue a confirmation pack and update your corporate file.
