Who It’s For

  • Cyprus companies needing VAT registration for trading, invoicing, or onboarding with counterparties
  • Groups with cross-border activity that need the correct VAT route mapped before filing
  • Businesses that want a clean file and predictable return calendar after registration

What You Get (Deliverables)

  • Eligibility confirmation and registration route recommendation (coordinated with your advisors)
  • Application pack preparation, submission receipts, and a correspondence log
  • Authority clarifications handled promptly to avoid rework
  • VAT certificate/number handover and confirmations for banks/auditors (on request)
  • Post-registration compliance calendar (returns, and related reporting where applicable)

Registration Options (At a Glance)

Option Typical Use Strengths Considerations
Domestic VAT Number Cyprus-based trading with local inputs/outputs Standard compliance baseline; bank/auditor familiarity Regular returns; additional reporting may apply
Non-Established Registration No fixed establishment, but taxable supplies in Cyprus Enables local charging/recovery where allowed Local representation or address may be needed
VAT Group (where eligible) Related entities seeking internal supply simplification Simplifies filings; potential cash-flow benefits Eligibility and governance requirements apply
EU One-Stop Shop schemes (where relevant) Cross-border sales to consumers in the EU Single filing for multiple EU states (scope-dependent) Scheme scope is specific — confirm with tax advisor

We outline viable routes and coordinate with your advisors to confirm the optimal path for your activity.

Process & Typical Timeline

  1. Scoping: confirm activity, supply chain, and which registration type fits.
  2. Evidence pack: compile supporting documents and align certifications where required.
  3. Build & submit: complete forms, submit, and log reference numbers.
  4. Clarifications: respond to authority queries and supply supplemental evidence.
  5. Issuance & handover: provide VAT number/certificate and set the initial compliance calendar.

FAQs

Informational only — technical VAT positions and thresholds are confirmed by your tax advisors.

How long does VAT registration take?

Timing depends on authority workload and the completeness of the file. We focus on clean submissions and quick responses to avoid rework.

Can we register voluntarily?

Often yes, depending on your facts and the applicable rules. We coordinate with your tax advisor to confirm eligibility and implications.

Will you notify our bank and auditor?

Yes — on request we send a confirmation pack and keep your corporate file updated.

Do you help with online portal access?

Where applicable, we can assist with activation and basic handover so your accountants can file returns smoothly.
