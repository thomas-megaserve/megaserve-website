Who It’s For

  • Companies that need VAT returns and related submissions filed on time with consistent working papers
  • Owners who want a predictable monthly/quarterly rhythm and clear cut-offs for inputs
  • Teams that want reconciliations and a clean archive ready for auditors and banking reviews

What You Get (Deliverables)

  • Period intake checklist and cut-off dates (what we need and by when)
  • Reconciliations (ledgers ↔ bank ↔ VAT control) with an exceptions list
  • Submission receipts (VAT/VIES) and payment references
  • Indexed “period pack” archive suitable for audits and banking queries
  • Rolling compliance calendar with reminders

Process & Typical Timeline

  1. Intake: receive ledger exports, bank statements, and supporting documents via secure channel.
  2. Reconcile: cross-checks, variance review, and an exceptions list for resolution.
  3. Prepare: draft returns/submissions and a short sign-off note (movements + assumptions).
  4. Submit: lodge via e-Services; issue receipts and payment references.
  5. Archive: store working papers and receipts in a structured period folder for audit.

Case Study

Clean Periodic VAT & VIES Filing with Reconciliations (Anonymised)

Client: Cyprus trading/services company with steady transaction volume (anonymised)

The client needed VAT returns filed on time, with a clean paper trail for auditors and bank reviews. The challenge was maintaining consistent inputs, reconciling movements each period, and avoiding avoidable authority queries.

MegaServe implemented a monthly cut-off and intake checklist, reconciled sales and purchase ledgers to bank activity and VAT control balances, prepared a short sign-off note showing key movements and assumptions, and then lodged submissions through the portal with payment references. Each period ended with an indexed “period pack” (receipts + working papers) stored for audit.

Result: on-time submissions with predictable workflows and an audit-ready archive that reduced last-minute scrambles and repeat queries.

FAQs

Do you replace our accountants?

No. We handle administration, reconciliations, submissions, and liaison. Tax technical positions remain with your advisors.

Can you help with late or corrective filings?

Yes. We assemble prior-period packs, coordinate adjustments with your accountants, and submit corrections per authority rules.

Will you manage payments?

We provide payment references and timing guidance. Execution remains with your authorised banking users under dual-control.

Can you work across multiple entities?

Yes. We maintain separate calendars and archives, and can share a consolidated status note.
