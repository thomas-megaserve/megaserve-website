Who It’s For

  • New Cyprus companies that need a tax number for banking, VAT, and ongoing filings
  • Existing companies that need updates after changes (director, UBO, registered office)
  • Individuals who need a personal TIC/TIN for banking or tax processes

What You Get

  • TIC/TIN confirmation and reference details
  • Copy of submitted forms and supporting documents
  • Authority correspondence saved and indexed
  • Bank/auditor confirmation pack (on request)
  • Optional: e-Services activation and handover notes for your tax advisor

Process & Typical Timeline

  1. Check documents: confirm the correct list for the company or person.
  2. Prepare forms: drafts + signatures, including any authorisations.
  3. Submit: lodge with the Tax Department and answer any questions.
  4. Confirm: deliver the TIC/TIN and the confirmation pack.

FAQs

What’s the difference between TIC and TIN?

Both terms refer to the taxpayer identification number used by the Cyprus Tax Department. We use TIC/TIN interchangeably for clarity.

Do directors or foreign shareholders need a personal TIC/TIN?

Sometimes. Banks or authorities may request it depending on activity and filings. We confirm this during intake.

Can you fast-track the number?

We cannot control authority queues, but we pre-check documents, follow correct formats, and answer clarifications quickly.

Will you share the TIC/TIN with our bank and auditor?

Yes. On request, we provide a confirmation pack and update your corporate records.
