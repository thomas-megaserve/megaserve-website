Who It’s For

  • Newly incorporated Cyprus companies that need a tax number for banking, VAT, and ongoing compliance
  • Existing companies that need updates after director/UBO or registered office changes
  • Individuals (directors/UBOs/investors) who require a personal TIC/TIN for banking or tax workflows

What You Get (Deliverables)

  • TIC/TIN confirmation and reference details
  • Copy of filed forms and supporting documents
  • Authority correspondence archived and indexed
  • Confirmation note/pack for banks, auditors, and counterparties (on request)
  • Optional: e-Services activation and handover notes for your tax advisor

Process & Typical Timeline

  1. Intake & Pre-check: verify entity/individual details and compile the document list.
  2. Drafting & Signatures: prepare forms and any authorisations; confirm certification formats.
  3. Submission: lodge with the Tax Department and handle clarifications quickly.
  4. Issuance & Confirmation: obtain the TIC/TIN and issue confirmations for banking and advisor handover.

MegaServe verified the company’s certificate set and signatory details, prepared the Tax Department registration file in the correct format, and handled submission and any clarifications. Once issued, we provided a confirmation pack for the bank and a clean handover for the VAT/tax advisor.

  • Authority queues: we can’t control processing time, but we reduce delays by submitting a clean, correctly formatted file.

  • Downstream dependencies: we coordinate sequencing with banks and VAT/tax advisors to avoid duplicated work.

FAQs

What’s the difference between TIC and TIN?

Both terms refer to a taxpayer identification number used by the Cyprus Tax Department. We use TIC/TIN interchangeably for clarity.

Do directors or foreign shareholders need a personal TIC/TIN?

Sometimes. Banks or authorities may request it depending on the activity profile and filings. We confirm this during intake.

Can you fast-track the number?

We can’t control authority queues, but we pre-check documents, format certifications properly, and respond to clarifications quickly to avoid delays.

Will you share the TIC/TIN with our bank and auditor?

Yes. On request we provide a confirmation pack and update your corporate records.
