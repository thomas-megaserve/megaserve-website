TIC / TIN Registration Services

Obtain a Cyprus Tax Identification Code (TIC/TIN) cleanly—documents prepared correctly, filings sequenced to avoid rework, and confirmations delivered to your bank, auditors, and advisors.

What We Handle

  • Entity TIC/TIN for newly incorporated Cyprus companies and branches
  • Individual TIC/TIN for directors, officers, and foreign shareholders where required
  • Updates to records (registered office, directors, contact details) to keep tax files current
  • Authority liaison and portal submissions; certified copies and translations if needed
  • e-Services activation (where applicable) and handover to your tax advisor

When You’ll Need It

  • Immediately post-incorporation (required by banks, auditors, and for VAT/CIT processes)
  • When opening or re-onboarding bank/EMI accounts
  • Before VAT registration or other tax filings
  • On director/UBO changes or a change of registered office

We coordinate with your accountants to ensure numbering aligns with downstream filings and reporting cycles.

Process & Typical Timeline

  1. Intake & Pre-check: verify eligibility, confirm entity/individual details, and compile document list.
  2. Drafting & Signatures: prepare forms, resolutions/PoAs if required, and certification instructions.
  3. Submission: lodge with the Tax Department; handle clarifications and any supplementary evidence.
  4. Issuance & Confirmation: obtain TIC/TIN, update your records, and send confirmations to banks/auditors.
  5. Optional Add-ons: e-Services activation, VAT registration handover, and calendar reminders.

Deliverables

  • TIC/TIN confirmation and reference details
  • Copy of filed forms and supporting documents
  • Authority correspondence archived and indexed
  • Update note for banks, auditors, and counterparties
  • Optional: e-Services login activation & handover guide

Documents Checklist

Entity (Company/Branch) Individual (Director/UBO/Investor)
  • Certificate set (Incorporation, Directors/Secretary, Shareholders, Registered Office)
  • Memorandum & Articles (latest)
  • Directors’ IDs/PoAs; company authorised signatory ID
  • Registered office proof (agreement/confirmation)
  • Contact details (emails, phones) and activity overview
  • Valid passport/ID (certified as required)
  • Recent proof of address (≤ 3 months)
  • Contact details and role/relationship to the entity
  • Local contact point (if applicable)

We’ll provide certification formats and samples to avoid resubmissions. Translations/apostilles arranged where required.

FAQ

What’s the difference between TIC and TIN?
Both terms refer to a taxpayer identification number used by the Cyprus Tax Department. We use TIC/TIN interchangeably for clarity.
Do directors or foreign shareholders need a personal TIC/TIN?
Sometimes—banks or authorities may request it depending on your activities and filings. We’ll confirm at intake.
Can you fast-track the number?
We can’t control authority queues, but we pre-check documents, format certifications properly, and respond to clarifications quickly to avoid delays.
Will you share the TIC/TIN with our bank and auditor?
Yes—on request we send a confirmation pack directly and update your corporate records.

Information here is general and not tax advice. We’re happy to work alongside your tax advisors.
