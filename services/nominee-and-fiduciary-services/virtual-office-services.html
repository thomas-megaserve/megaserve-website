Who It’s For

  • Owners who want a Cyprus contact presence without renting a full-time office
  • Companies that need reliable mail and courier handling
  • Teams that need meeting room access during visits to Cyprus

What You Get

  • Business address for everyday use (website, invoices, correspondence)
  • Local phone number + call answering and message forwarding
  • Mail received, scanned, and forwarded; couriers arranged with tracking
  • Meeting room bookings in Nicosia (hourly / half-day / full day)
  • Reception support for visitors
  • Admin support when needed (printing, document packs, certified copy coordination)
  • Optional add-ons via Registered Address and Physical Office

Process & Typical Timeline

Onboarding Flow

  1. Intake: contacts, forwarding rules, call script (if any), and access emails.
  2. Set-up: phone number, mailbox, shared folder, and escalation rules.
  3. Test: test call + test scan + a sample courier dispatch (if needed).
  4. Go-live: agree the monthly cadence and meeting room access.

Requirements (Onboarding)

  • Company details (name, registration number, key contacts)
  • Forwarding emails/addresses and escalation rules
  • Call script (if applicable) and routing instructions
  • Any confidentiality rules for scans
  • Meeting room needs (capacity, equipment)

Practical Notes

  • Business address vs registered office: Virtual Office is for business use. For the legal registered office, use Registered Address.
  • Local presence: Virtual Office helps with mail and calls. If you need meetings, office space, or stronger presence, add Directorship and/or Physical Office.
  • Call content: we do not provide legal/tax advice on calls; we take messages and route them as agreed.
  • Data handling: scans are stored in a structured folder with access controls; retention can be set.

FAQs

Can the virtual office be used as the company’s registered office?

No. The registered office is a separate legal service. See Registered Address.

Do you provide a local phone number and answer calls in Greek/English?

Yes. We provide a local number and answer in English/Greek (Russian on request), forwarding messages based on your script.

How quickly do you forward urgent mail?

Urgent items are flagged on intake and can be dispatched the same day if confirmed before 14:00.

Can we combine this with meeting rooms and stronger local presence?

Yes. Combine with Directorship (board meetings in Cyprus) and/or Physical Office for dedicated space.

If you have unusual requirements (after-hours calls, large consignments, extra confidentiality), include them in your email and we’ll tailor the setup.
