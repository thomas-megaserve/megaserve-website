Who It’s For

  • International companies needing practical Cyprus premises to support substance expectations and local operations
  • Teams that require meeting rooms, reception, and secure connectivity without long lease commitments
  • Owners who want a clean, bankable setup integrated with governance and corporate services

What You Get (Deliverables)

  • Move-in-ready office or dedicated desk with flexible terms
  • Meeting room access (hourly/half-day/day) and reception support
  • Business-grade connectivity (wired + Wi-Fi), with optional enhanced IT requests
  • Visitor logging and optional evidence trails for bank/audit comfort
  • Mail & courier handling integrated with your administration workflows
  • Optional integration with Registered Address and Virtual Office

Process & Typical Timeline

  1. Discovery: headcount, privacy/IT needs, meeting cadence, signage, budget.
  2. Shortlist & Viewings: 2–3 suitable options with plan/photos/specs; site tours if needed.
  3. Terms & Docs: licence/lease draft, KYC, insurance certificates (if applicable).
  4. Fit-Out & IT: furniture plan, VLAN/static IP (optional), access badges, test prints/VC.
  5. Move-In: handover checklist, welcome pack, building rules, escalation contacts.

Case Study

Rapid Move-In Office Setup Supporting Substance and Bank/Audit Comfort

Client: International client with a Cyprus entity (anonymised)

The client required a physical Cyprus office quickly to support operational substance expectations and to handle in-person meetings with banks, auditors, and counterparties. MegaServe coordinated the office selection, onboarding documentation, access setup, and practical operational details (meeting room availability, reception flow, and day-to-day readiness), aligned with governance and corporate services where needed.

Result: the client established a credible on-the-ground presence with a functional workspace and predictable meeting capability, supporting smoother third-party interactions and operational continuity.

Requirements (Client Checklist)

  • Company details (name, registration no., contact matrix)
  • Headcount by room/desk and privacy requirements
  • IT brief (VLAN/static IP, device policy, conferencing needs)
  • Signage/branding preferences (if any)
  • Insurance certificates (where required by landlord)

Risk & Mitigations

  • Availability: inventory and pricing depend on building occupancy; we shortlist alternatives to avoid delays.
  • Substance expectations: premises alone may not satisfy banks/auditors—governance and records matter; we coordinate directorship/secretarial if needed.
  • IT changes: VLAN/static IP/cabling requests may require vendor approvals; we set expectations upfront.
  • Access control: badges are individual; shared credentials aren’t permitted; logs can be maintained where required.

FAQs

Can you provide board-meeting suitable rooms and evidence?

Yes. We can host in-person meetings, maintain attendance records, and coordinate minutes via our governance services.

Do you handle IT hardening (VLAN, static IP, device rules)?

Yes. We coordinate with building vendors to set up VLAN, static IPs, MAC registration, and conference kit as required.

What lease terms are available?

Licences for smaller offices/desks and short-form leases for larger suites. We propose options matched to your timeline and budget.

Can we bundle Registered Address and Virtual Office?

Yes. Most clients combine physical offices with Registered Address and a light Virtual Office plan for after-hours coverage.

Unusual requirements (secure storage, 24/7 access, enhanced privacy)? Add them to your inquiry—we’ll reflect them in the shortlist.
