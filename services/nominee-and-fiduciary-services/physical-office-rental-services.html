Who It’s For

  • International companies that need office space in Cyprus for day-to-day work and meetings
  • Teams that want meeting rooms, reception, and reliable internet without a long lease
  • Owners who want office space that can be combined with governance and corporate support

What You Get

  • Move-in-ready office or dedicated desk with flexible terms
  • Meeting room access (hourly / half-day / full day) and reception support
  • Business internet (wired + Wi-Fi), with IT add-ons available on request
  • Visitor logging if needed for banks/auditors
  • Mail and courier handling linked to your admin workflow
  • Optional integration with Registered Address and Virtual Office

Process & Typical Timeline

  1. Discovery: headcount, privacy/IT needs, meeting needs, signage, budget.
  2. Shortlist & viewings: 2–3 options with photos/specs; site visits if needed.
  3. Terms & documents: licence/lease draft, KYC, and insurance (if required).
  4. Setup: furniture, access, internet/IT options, test printing/video calls.
  5. Move-in: handover checklist, building rules, and support contacts.

Amenities & Options

  • Reception, visitor logging, guest Wi-Fi
  • Meeting rooms (hourly/half-day/day), video-conference kit
  • Business internet (wired + Wi-Fi), optional dedicated bandwidth
  • Printing/scanning, mail & courier handling
  • Kitchenette, coffee/water, cleaning, utilities
  • Security in common areas (CCTV), key cards/FOBs; private lockups on request
  • IT options: VLAN, static IP, device registration, cabling changes (on request)
  • Branding: door plaque/signage (subject to building policy)

Availability and amenities vary by building; we confirm everything before you commit.

Requirements

  • Company details (name, registration number, contact person)
  • Headcount and privacy requirements
  • IT needs (VLAN/static IP, device policy, video calls)
  • Signage/branding preference (if any)
  • Insurance certificates (only if the landlord requires them)

Risk & Notes

  • Availability: space and pricing depend on occupancy; we always provide backups.
  • What banks/auditors expect: office space helps, but records and governance also matter; we can coordinate directorship/secretarial if needed.
  • IT changes: VLAN/static IP/cabling may require vendor approval; we set expectations early.
  • Access control: access badges are individual; shared logins are not acceptable.

FAQs

Can you provide meeting rooms suitable for board meetings?

Yes. We can arrange meeting rooms and, where needed, keep basic attendance notes and coordinate minutes via our governance services.

Can you help with IT setup (VLAN, static IP, device rules)?

Yes. We coordinate with building vendors for VLAN, static IPs, device registration, and video-conference setup when required.

What lease terms are available?

Smaller spaces usually use flexible licences. Larger suites can use short-form leases. We propose options that match your timeline.

Can we bundle Registered Address and Virtual Office?

Yes. Many clients combine an office with Registered Address and a light Virtual Office plan.

If you need secure storage, 24/7 access, or extra privacy, include it in your email and we’ll shortlist accordingly.
