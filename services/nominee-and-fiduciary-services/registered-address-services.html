Registered Address Services

We provide a Cyprus registered office address and handle official mail. We log what arrives, scan or forward it based on your rules, and keep clear records.

Nicosia-based · Secure mail handling · Clear tracking
Email Us Start Now

Note: A registered address is a legal requirement, but it is not the same as a real office. If you need desk space, meeting rooms, or staff support, see Physical Office Rental and Virtual Office.

Who it’s for What you get Process Case study Requirements Risk FAQs Start

Who It’s For

  • Cyprus companies that need a legal registered office address and reliable mail handling
  • Owners who want clear rules for bank and government letters, and for original documents
  • Groups with multiple companies that need separate logging and clear organisation

What You Get

  • Registered office address in Cyprus + required company display (name plate)
  • Mail received and logged (date/time), then scanned or forwarded based on your rules
  • Courier handling for originals, with tracking and delivery proof
  • Optional storage of originals, with an inventory list and checkout record
  • Bank/government letters organised with a searchable index
  • Optional coordination with Corporate Secretarial for filings and registers

Process & Typical Timeline

  1. Intake: company details, directors/UBOs, mail rules, forwarding details.
  2. Setup/change: set the registered office (if changing), and prepare any bank/government notifications.
  3. Signage: install the name plate and confirm the mail rules.
  4. Go-live: mail handling begins; optional originals storage inventory created.

Mail Handling

  • We record sender/recipient and flag urgent or secure items
  • Scans are saved as date-stamped PDFs; sensitive items handled based on your rules
  • Forwarding can be weekly, express, or “trigger-based” (e.g., any bank letter)
  • Digital index is kept; physical items stored per your policy (default 12 months)
  • Access is limited to named contacts

Requirements

  • Certificate set (Incorporation, Directors/Secretary, Shareholders, Registered Office)
  • Memorandum & Articles and current registers
  • Authorised contacts and forwarding details
  • Your mail rules (scan/forward schedule + sensitive mail rule)
  • Any bank/government templates for address change (if needed)

Risk & Notes

  • Bank/auditor expectations: some banks/auditors may also want meetings or a real office, depending on your activity.
  • Sensitive mail: by default, we handle sensitive items carefully unless you approve wider scanning.
  • Courier timing: depends on courier schedules and (where relevant) customs.
  • Access control: we restrict access to named contacts and keep a handling log.

FAQs

Is a registered office enough for banking and auditors?

It meets the legal requirement. Some banks and auditors may also want proof of real activity (meetings, office, staff). We can help add these services if needed.

Can multiple companies use the same registered office?

Yes. We can keep separate mail logs and separate document folders for each company.

What happens with sensitive mail?

You choose the rule: scan everything, scan only covers, or do not scan. Sensitive items default to “no scan” unless you approve scanning.

Do you store originals?

Yes. If you choose storage, we keep an inventory list and a checkout record for any originals taken out.

How fast can you switch our registered office?

Often within 2–5 business days once we have the documents and approvals. Bank/government updates can take longer depending on the institution.
