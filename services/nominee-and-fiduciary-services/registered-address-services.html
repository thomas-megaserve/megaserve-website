Who It’s For

  • Cyprus entities needing a compliant registered office with reliable mail handling and documented trails
  • International owners who want clear routing rules for bank/authority correspondence and originals
  • Groups with multiple entities that need indexed, entity-separated handling and custody options

What You Get (Deliverables)

  • Registered office address in Cyprus with statutory display (name plate)
  • Mail receipt + logging (timestamped) and scanning/forwarding per your rules
  • Courier management for originals; tracked dispatch with delivery proofs
  • Optional custody: originals storage, inventory list, and chain-of-custody checkouts
  • Authority/bank correspondence handling with a searchable index
  • Integration with Corporate Secretarial for filings and registers

Process & Typical Timeline

  1. Intake: entity details, directors/UBOs, mail rules, forwarding addresses.
  2. Resolutions & updates: registered office setup/change (if applicable) and bank/authority notifications.
  3. Signage & setup: name plate installed; mail rules tested; index created.
  4. Go-live: mail handling begins; custody (if selected) inventory created.

Mail Handling & Compliance

  • Intake protocol with sender/recipient capture; oversized/secured items flagged
  • Scanning standards: searchable PDFs, date-stamped; sensitive items by exception only
  • Forwarding rules: weekly batch, ad hoc express, or on-trigger (e.g., “bank”, “authority”)
  • Retention: digital index kept; physical items stored per your policy (default 12 months)
  • GDPR: access limited to your named contacts; encryption at-rest and in transit
  • Statutory display: company name and number; signage maintained and photographed on install

Requirements (Client Checklist)

  • Certificate set (Incorporation, Directors/Secretary, Shareholders, Registered Office)
  • Memorandum & Articles; current registers
  • Authorised contacts & forwarding addresses
  • Mail rules (scan/forward cadence + sensitive item handling)
  • Any bank/authority templates for address change (if required)

Risk & Mitigations

  • Substance expectations: registered office meets statutory requirements; banks/auditors may require governance/premises depending on profile.
  • Sensitive mail handling: defaults to controlled scanning unless you authorise broader scanning rules.
  • Courier timing: dispatch times depend on courier schedules and (where applicable) customs/clearance.
  • Access control: we restrict access to named contacts and log handling activity for audit trail.

FAQs

Is the registered office enough for banking and auditors?

It meets statutory requirements. Some banks/auditors may expect substance (board activity, premises, staff). We can coordinate governance and premises if needed.

Can multiple entities share the same registered office?

Yes, with clear mail rules and separate indices. Group discounts may apply depending on volume.

What happens with sensitive mail?

We follow your rules: scan subject/cover only, scan nothing, or scan everything. Sensitive items default to “no scan” unless authorised.

Do you hold minute books and originals?

Yes. Dedicated custody provides an inventory and chain-of-custody receipts for checkouts.

How fast can you switch our registered office?

Often within 2–5 business days from receipt of documents and approvals; banks/authorities updates may vary by institution.
