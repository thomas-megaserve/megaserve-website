Overview

Nominee & fiduciary services should be treated as governance infrastructure—not box-ticking. We provide documented, independent roles and operational support designed to withstand bank, audit, and counterparty scrutiny.

Who It’s For

  • Owners and groups who require Cyprus resident governance with documented decision-making
  • Companies needing a reliable company secretary function and clean statutory registers
  • Structures requiring registered office and mail handling with controlled access
  • Teams that want office/meeting capability without committing to long leases

How We Work

  1. Scope: confirm objectives (banking, governance, substance), parties, and risk constraints.
  2. Mandates: define authority matrix, approvals, roles, and decision cadence.
  3. Execution: implement appointments/filings, records control, and correspondence routing.
  4. Maintenance: calendar-based renewals, access control, and quarterly status notes where applicable.

FAQs

Are nominee services “rubber-stamp” arrangements?

No. We operate within documented board-approved mandates and maintain decision records. Independence and auditability matter.

Can you coordinate with our lawyers, auditors, and accountants?

Yes. We coordinate documents and timing; we do not replace regulated advisors.

Do you provide registered office and mail handling?

Yes. Registered Address provides statutory compliance plus secure handling rules and audit-ready trails.

Can we combine governance and premises?

Yes. Many clients combine Directorship with meeting space or office rental to satisfy substance expectations.
