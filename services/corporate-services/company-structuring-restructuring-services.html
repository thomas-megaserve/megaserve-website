Who It’s For

  • Groups requiring a clean holding/trading spine across subsidiaries, branches, and SPVs
  • Companies facing banking friction, de-risking requirements, or payment corridor instability
  • Owners preparing for acquisition, divestment, investor demands, or a governance uplift

What You Get (Deliverables)

  • Structural options memo (diagram + decision brief)
  • Implementation plan (Gantt-style checkpoints, critical path, owners)
  • Registrar packs, resolutions, intercompany agreements, policy templates
  • Banking dossier (KYC package, activity narrative, cashflow map)
  • Compliance calendar (tax/VAT, UBO/BoR, filings, renewals)
  • Substance plan (roles, office, VO, vendors, IT stack)

Process & Typical Timeline

  1. Discovery: commercial goals, current map, counterparty & banking constraints, risk profile.
  2. Blueprint: 2–3 viable patterns with pros/cons, tax/compliance notes, time/cost, dependencies.
  3. Execution: registrar filings, intercompany contracts, banking onboarding, VAT/TIC, HR/substance.
  4. Stabilise: first-90-days monitoring, filings calendar, governance cadence, periodic tune-ups.

Case Study

Multi-Entity Group Structuring (Oil & Gas Services)

Client: International oil & gas services group (anonymised)

An international oil & gas services group required a Cyprus-aligned structure capable of supporting multiple subsidiaries, branches, and registered offices, while remaining credible to banks and compliant with ongoing regulatory obligations.

MegaServe mapped the existing group, produced a target structure and governance framework, coordinated registered office and statutory alignments, and executed the required corporate actions and registrar filings end-to-end. The restructuring was completed in less than standard Cyprus timelines, with no registrar rework or resubmissions required, resulting in a clean, bankable group structure suitable for ongoing operations and compliance.

Requirements (Client Checklist)

  • Group org chart & cap table (current & desired)
  • Recent financials & key contracts (intercompany, major suppliers/customers)
  • Directors’ IDs/POAs; UBO IDs/POAs; proof of address
  • Business activity narrative & projected cashflows
  • Existing bank details, pain points, onboarding feedback (if any)

Risk & Mitigations

  • Banking cadence: onboarding timelines are subject to bank/EMI due diligence; we standardise the KYC narrative and cashflow map to reduce rework.
  • Multi-entity coordination: we run an actions register (owners/dates/dependencies) to prevent missed filings during transition.
  • Substance expectations: we phase substance build-out where required (directors/office/vendors) to keep the structure credible to counterparties.
  • Cyprus levers: efficient holding/trading hub positioning for CEE/MEA flows; broad treaty network; familiar EU compliance baseline; predictable corporate maintenance cadence.

FAQs

Can you support cross-border re-domiciliations?

Yes. We coordinate counsel across sending/receiving registrars, migrate statutory books, and align banking & tax/VAT.

Do you provide directors and local office?

Yes. We can provide nominee directors, registered address, and physical/virtual office options with appropriate controls.

What about banking if a file has history?

We prepare a banking narrative that addresses historic issues directly, updates KYC data, and maps clean payment flows.

How do you ensure ongoing compliance?

We install a filings calendar, policy set (KYC/AML, sanctions), and light-touch quarterly checks or an annual review.

If you have an unusual jurisdiction or complex legacy, include a short background when you contact us. We’ll factor it into the first options memo.
