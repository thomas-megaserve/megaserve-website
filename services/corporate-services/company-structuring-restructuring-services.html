Company Structuring & Re-Structuring

Design or refine Cyprus-linked structures—holding, trading, SPVs—aligned with governance, tax efficiency, banking, and operational risk. We blueprint options, cost them, and deliver filings, contracts, banking, and substance—cleanly.

What We Do

  • Holding & trading architectures; IP/SPV vehicles; branch vs subsidiary analysis
  • Cross-border re-domiciliation, share capital changes, intercompany realignments
  • Board composition, governance policies, decision-rights, and delegations
  • Substance build-out: directors, staff, registered/physical office, VO/IT stack
  • Banking footprint optimisation (Cyprus & EEA/UK/CH/ROW), payments pathways
  • Compliance scaffolding: KYC/AML, sanctions, tax/VAT registrations, reporting

When to Re-Structure

  • Acquisition, divestment, or group carve-out requires a new legal & tax spine
  • Banking friction, de-risking, or payment corridor instability
  • Shareholder changes, investor demands, or governance uplift
  • Entering new markets; VAT/TIC & permanent establishment considerations
  • Cost discipline: simplify entities, reduce advisors, automate reporting

We emphasise operationally bankable designs (i.e., credible to counterparties and banks)—not just diagrams.

Our Approach

  1. Discovery: commercial goals, current map, counterparty & banking constraints, risk profile.
  2. Blueprint: 2–3 viable patterns with pros/cons, tax/compliance notes, time/cost, dependencies.
  3. Execution: registrar filings, intercompany contracts, banking onboarding, VAT/TIC, HR/substance.
  4. Stabilise: first-90-days monitoring, filings calendar, governance cadence, periodic tune-ups.

Deliverables

  • Structural options memo (diagram + decision brief)
  • Implementation plan (Gantt-style checkpoints, critical path, owners)
  • Registrar packs, resolutions, intercompany agreements, policy templates
  • Banking dossier (KYC package, activity narrative, cashflow map)
  • Compliance calendar (tax/VAT, UBO/BoR, filings, renewals)
  • Substance plan (roles, office, VO, vendors, IT stack)

Cyprus-Specific Levers

  • Efficient holding/trading hub positioning for CEE/MEA flows
  • Broad treaty network and familiar EU compliance baseline
  • Well-trodden registrar and banking practices for corporates
  • Predictable corporate maintenance and reporting cadence

We can integrate non-Cyprus entities (EEA/UK/CH/UA/AE/US) into a coherent, bankable chain where needed.

Typical Scenarios We Execute

  • Group Clean-Up: merge dormant entities, regularise share capital, and reduce annual costs.
  • Banking Reset: pivot to alternative EMIs/banks; update narratives and documentation to pass onboarding.
  • Expansion: add VAT registrations and PE controls for new markets without over-creating entities.
  • Governance Uplift: re-compose boards, institute decision-rights/policy stack, improve audit readiness.
  • M&A Integration: align target with group chart, contracts, reporting, and cash management.

Documents Checklist (Typical)

  • Group org chart & cap table (current & desired)

FAQ

Can you support cross-border re-domiciliations?
Yes. We coordinate counsel across sending/receiving registrars, migrate statutory books, and align banking & tax/VAT.
Do you provide directors and local office?
Yes. We can provide nominee directors, registered address, and physical/virtual office options with appropriate controls.
What about banking if a file has history?
We prepare a banking narrative that addresses historic issues directly, updates KYC data, and maps clean payment flows.
How do you ensure ongoing compliance?
We install a filings calendar, policy set (KYC/AML, sanctions), and light-touch quarterly checks or an annual review.

If you have an unusual jurisdiction or complex legacy, include a short background when you contact us. We’ll factor it into the first options memo.
