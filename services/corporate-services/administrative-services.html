Who It’s For

  • Cyprus companies that need reliable day-to-day administration and clean statutory records
  • Groups managing multiple Cyprus entities requiring calendars, registers, and predictable response times
  • Owners who want audit-ready documentation for banks, auditors, counterparties, and authorities

What You Get (Deliverables)

  • Corporate record-keeping & minute books (digital + physical, where required)
  • Board & shareholder resolutions, PoAs, incumbency & good-standing docs
  • Registrar updates (officers, UBO/BoR, share capital), annual returns & renewals
  • Liaison with banks/EMIs, auditors, notaries, and authorities (Tax/VAT/Registrar)
  • Certifications, apostilles, legalised translations, couriering and document routing
  • Register management: directors, shareholders, charges, significant controllers
  • KYC refresh cycles, policy implementation, and light compliance housekeeping
  • Annual filings & renewals calendar with reminders
  • Authority correspondences archived and indexed
  • Banking dossier folder (KYC basics, activity narrative, signatory map)
  • Quarterly status note (open items, risks, upcoming renewals)

Process & Typical Timeline

  1. Intake: short call to map entities, signatories, banks, auditors, and current issues.
  2. Data Room: we create a secure folder structure and import key corporate records.
  3. Access: read-only access to banking/auditor portals where applicable (or proxy via PDFs).
  4. Calendar: we build a filings/renewals calendar (annual return, UBO/BoR, VAT/TIC, licenses).
  5. Templates: resolution/PoA templates aligned to your decision rights and governance style.
  6. Go-Live: confirm points of contact; begin handling daily requests under agreed targets.

Case Study

Statutory Audit Support for Complex Administrative Operations

Client: International wind energy group with a Cyprus operating entity (anonymised)

The client’s Cyprus entity formed part of a wider international wind energy operation with a complex operational and governance footprint. Despite the audit being statutory in nature, the structure and volume of underlying corporate records required disciplined, up-to-date administration.

MegaServe maintained current statutory registers, resolutions, and filings, and provided auditors with a structured and indexed documentation set on request.

Result: the statutory audit proceeded without hindrance or delay attributable to corporate administration.

Requirements (Client Checklist)

  • Certificate set (Incorporation, Directors/Secretary, Shareholders, Registered Office)
  • Memorandum & Articles, current registers, minute book snapshots
  • IDs/PoAs for directors and UBOs; proof of address
  • Latest annual return and any recent registrar filings
  • Auditor details, bank details, and recent KYC requests (if any)

Risk & Mitigations

  • Incomplete records: we reconstruct minute books and registers from registrar records and historical documents.
  • Third-party timelines: registrar/authority queues can drive turnaround; we track progress and provide status notes until closure.
  • Repeat KYC requests: we align refresh cycles to bank/auditor calendars and keep KYC packs standardised to reduce repetition.
  • Document routing: certifications/apostilles/couriers are handled with tracked delivery proofs and archived receipts.
