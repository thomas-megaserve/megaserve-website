Who It’s For

  • Cyprus companies that need bank/EMI onboarding support and clean KYC/KYB dossiers
  • International groups requiring a credible Cyprus/EU banking footprint with documented payment flows
  • Owners who want predictable reviews, user controls, and ongoing relationship management without rework

What You Get (Deliverables)

  • Banking shortlist with pros/cons, dependencies, and sequence of applications
  • KYC/KYB dossier ready for upload (structured file tree + PDFs)
  • Activity narrative and payment flow diagram for compliance reviews
  • Signatory matrix & user access plan (roles, limits, dual-control options)
  • Merchant/PSP intake pack and gateway settings checklist
  • Banking maintenance calendar (reviews, attestations, card/limit refresh dates)

Process & Typical Timeline

  1. Fit Assessment: activity, jurisdictions, counterparties, projected volumes, and beneficial owners.
  2. Dossier Build: corporate certificates, registers, IDs/PoAs, activity narrative, flow & corridor map.
  3. Submission & Interviews: portal forms, clarifications, compliance interviews, risk notes.
  4. Activation: user setup, signatory matrix, limits, 2FA, test payments, card & FX settings.

Case Study

Payment Administration for Time-Critical Maritime Operations

Client: International ship repair and maintenance company with a Cyprus entity and Cyprus bank accounts (anonymised)

The client’s operations required reliable, time-critical supplier and service payments, with clear documentation and consistent processing to avoid disruption. MegaServe maintained the payments administration workflow: compiling supporting documents, preparing payment instructions, coordinating bank requirements and references, and ensuring approvals and records were properly archived for audit and reconciliation.

Result: time-critical payments were processed predictably with a documented trail, supporting uninterrupted operations.

Requirements (Client Checklist)

  • Certificate set, registers, Memorandum & Articles, minute book snapshots
  • UBO/director IDs & PoAs; proof of address; CVs if requested
  • Business activity narrative; counterparties & jurisdictions list
  • Projected volumes (monthly), currencies, and example invoices/contracts
  • Existing accounts and pain points (if any); auditor details

Risk & Mitigations

  • No guarantees: approvals and terms remain at the discretion of banks/EMIs/acquirers; we strengthen the dossier and sequence applications to reduce friction.
  • EDD cadence: enhanced due diligence can extend timelines; we prepare supporting evidence up front and track clarifications to closure.
  • Controls: we document user roles, limits, and dual-control options to reduce operational and audit risk.
  • Review cycles: we maintain a banking maintenance calendar (KYC refresh, attestations, letters, limit reviews) to avoid last-minute scrambles.

FAQs

Can you guarantee account opening?

No firm can guarantee approvals. We position the file, sequence applications, and escalate appropriately to maximise acceptance.

Do you work with both banks and EMIs?

Yes. We maintain options across Cyprus/EU banks and reputable EMIs to balance credibility, speed, and feature set.

What if our business has historical issues?

We prepare a candid compliance narrative, address past items directly, and propose clean payment flows and controls.

Can you support card acquiring and PSP setup?

Yes. We prepare merchant packs, map MCCs, and coordinate with PSPs/gateways for a smooth go-live.

Share any unique aspects early (jurisdictions, counterparties, products). We’ll reflect them in the shortlist and narrative.
