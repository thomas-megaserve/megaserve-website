Banking Administration Services

We help you open and manage bank and EMI accounts in Cyprus and the EU. We prepare the bank file (KYC/KYB), support onboarding, set up user access, and help with reviews.

Who It’s For

  • Cyprus companies that need help opening bank/EMI accounts and preparing KYC/KYB files
  • International groups that need a usable Cyprus/EU banking setup with clear money flows
  • Owners who want clear user controls, predictable reviews, and less back-and-forth

What You Get

  • Shortlist of banks/EMIs with pros/cons and a clear application order
  • Bank file (KYC/KYB) prepared for upload in a structured folder
  • Simple activity summary + payment flow diagram for compliance teams
  • Signatory and user access plan (roles, limits, dual-control options)
  • Merchant/PSP intake pack and gateway settings checklist (if needed)
  • Bank calendar for reviews, letters, and periodic updates

Process & Typical Timeline

  1. Fit check: activity, countries involved, owners, and expected volumes.
  2. File build: certificates, registers, IDs/POAs, activity summary, flow map.
  3. Submission: applications, clarifications, and compliance calls if requested.
  4. Activation: user setup, limits, 2FA, test payments, cards and FX settings.

Footprint Options

Channel Typical Use Strengths Considerations
Cyprus / EU Bank Main operating account; payroll; supplier payments Strong payment rails; trusted by auditors and counterparties Stricter onboarding; formal review cycles
EU EMI (E-money) Multi-currency collections; faster onboarding (in many cases) Speed; API tools; virtual IBANs Limits/features vary; tiering and settlement rules apply
Merchant Acquirer / PSP Card processing and gateway routing Tools for risk and reporting MCC fit; chargebacks; reserves/rolling terms

Final approval and terms are always the bank/EMI/acquirer’s decision. We prepare the file to reduce delays and rework.

Case Study

Payment Administration for Time-Critical Maritime Operations

Client: International ship repair and maintenance company with a Cyprus entity and Cyprus bank accounts (anonymised)

The client needed reliable supplier and service payments, with clear supporting documents and predictable processing.

MegaServe organised payment support documents, prepared payment instructions, coordinated bank requirements, and kept records for audit and reconciliation.

Result: time-critical payments were processed on time with a clear paper trail.

Requirements

  • Certificate set, registers, Memorandum & Articles, minute book snapshot
  • UBO/director IDs and POAs; proof of address (and CVs if a bank requests)
  • Activity summary; list of key customers/suppliers and countries
  • Expected monthly volumes, currencies, and sample invoices/contracts
  • Existing accounts and pain points (if any); auditor details

Risk & Mitigations

  • No guarantees: banks/EMIs decide approvals and terms. We prepare a clean file and apply in a sensible order.
  • Extra due diligence: some cases take longer. We prepare supporting evidence early and track questions to closure.
  • User controls: we document roles, limits, and dual-control to reduce operational and audit risk.
  • Review cycles: we keep a calendar for KYC refresh, letters, and limit reviews to avoid last-minute problems.

FAQs

Can you guarantee account opening?

No. We prepare and position the file, but the bank/EMI decides.

Do you work with both banks and EMIs?

Yes. We use both, depending on your activity and needs.

What if our business has issues in the past?

We write a clear explanation, update documents, and show clean money flows and controls.

Can you support card acquiring and PSP setup?

Yes. We can prepare the merchant file and coordinate with PSPs/gateways.

If anything is unusual (countries, products, counterparties), tell us early. It changes which banks are suitable.
