How We Work

  1. Scope: we confirm what you need and what documents are required.
  2. Checklist: we give you a simple list and collect documents in an organised folder.
  3. Execution: we prepare filings and documents and keep proofs of what was submitted.
  4. Ongoing support: where needed, we track renewals and keep records updated.

FAQs

Can you provide more than one service together?

Yes. Many clients start with company formation or structuring and then add ongoing administration and banking support.

Do you work with our auditors, lawyers, and accountants?

Yes. We coordinate documents and timelines. We do not replace regulated advisors.

Can you help if our corporate records are incomplete?

Yes. We can rebuild registers and minute books from registrar records and historic documents, then correct the position.

Can you prepare documents for bank requests?

Yes. We keep organised records that can be shared with banks and auditors when they ask.
