How We Work

  1. Scope: we confirm the service fit, dependencies, and your end goal (banking, counterparties, governance).
  2. Checklist: we issue an evidence pack checklist and collect documents in a structured format.
  3. Execution: filings, records, liaison, and document issuance with a controlled audit trail.
  4. Maintenance: calendar-based renewals, change control, and quarterly status notes (where applicable).

FAQs

Can you bundle multiple corporate services into one engagement?

Yes. Many clients start with formation/structuring and then retain ongoing admin and banking coordination as a single operational scope.

Do you work alongside our auditors, lawyers, and accountants?

Yes. We coordinate documents and timelines; we do not replace regulated advisors.

Can you help if our records are incomplete?

Yes. We can reconstruct registers and minute books from registrar records and historic documentation, then regularise the position.

Do you provide “bank-ready” document packs?

Yes. We maintain clean, indexed records suitable for KYB requests, audit queries, and counterpart due diligence.
