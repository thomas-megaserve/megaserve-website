  • Authority queues: decision timing is controlled externally; we keep status clear and follow up to closure.
  • Income evidence clarity: we structure income proofs to avoid ambiguity and reduce back-and-forth.

FAQs

Informational only — this is not legal or immigration advice. Final decisions rest with competent authorities.

Who qualifies for the Digital Nomad Visa in Cyprus?

Remote workers earning income from outside Cyprus with sufficient income, suitable accommodation, valid insurance, and clean records.

Can I work for Cypriot employers on this visa?

No. This route is for remote work with an employer or business established outside Cyprus.

How long does preparation and decision take?

Preparation typically takes a few weeks depending on document readiness and legalisation. Decision times are set by authorities and vary by season and workload.

Can family members join me?

Often yes, subject to route rules and evidence. We confirm eligibility at scoping and coordinate the dependent documentation.

Do my documents need legalisation or apostille?

Many foreign-issued documents require legalisation/apostille and certified translation. We provide country-specific guidance during intake.
