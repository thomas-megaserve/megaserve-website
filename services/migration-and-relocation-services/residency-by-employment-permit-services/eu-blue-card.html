EU Blue Card — Cyprus

Highly-skilled permits with salary thresholds and degree/experience criteria. We scope eligibility, prepare filings, and coordinate onboarding with an audit-ready record trail.

Overview

The EU Blue Card enables non-EU highly skilled professionals to live and work in Cyprus under enhanced mobility and family re-grouping rights. MegaServe maps the role against current thresholds, validates qualifications and experience, builds the application, and manages every authority touchpoint.

  • Eligibility memo: role mapping, salary threshold, degree/experience checks
  • Jurisdiction-specific checklist, templates, and certified translation guidance
  • End-to-end filing & liaison with authorities, appointment coordination
  • Family member add-ons and post-approval onboarding
  • Renewal scheduling, reminders, and immutable audit trail

Process

  1. Discovery & role mapping — confirm Blue Card route, salary band, timing.
  2. Checklist issue — gather degree/experience proofs; legalisation/translation where needed.
  3. Application build — forms, employer attestations, evidence pack.
  4. Submission & liaison — file, track, handle authority queries and appointments.
  5. Decision & collections — approvals, biometrics/permit issuance as applicable.
  6. Onboarding — Social Insurance, TIC/TIN, payroll & banking coordination.
  7. Renewals — reminders and long-term compliance calendar.

FAQ

Who qualifies for the EU Blue Card in Cyprus?
Highly-skilled non-EU professionals with recognised qualifications or equivalent experience and a contract at/above the applicable salary threshold.
How long does preparation and decision take?
Preparation typically takes 3–7 weeks depending on document readiness and legalisation. Decision times are set by authorities and vary by season and workload.
Do my foreign documents need legalisation or apostille?
Yes, most foreign-issued documents require apostille or equivalent legalisation plus certified translation. We provide country-specific guidance.
Can family members join the principal applicant?
Yes, eligible dependants may apply on linked routes. We confirm eligibility at scoping and coordinate their filings end-to-end.
What if my salary or role changes after filing?
Material changes can impact eligibility and may require an update or re-filing. Notify us immediately for a compliant course of action.
