Who It’s For

  • Cyprus employers hiring non-EU staff for standard roles
  • International groups moving staff to Cyprus and needing clean documents and appointment planning
  • Employers who want renewals and changes tracked with organised records

What You Get

  • Route confirmation (what route, and why)
  • A clear document list for the employer, employee, and (if needed) family
  • Prepared forms and a submission record (what was filed and when)
  • Biometrics booking support and attendance notes
  • Permit copies and renewal reminders
  • Clean records for banks, auditors, and future renewals

Process & Typical Timeline

  1. Route check: we confirm the right route and any risks.
  2. Collect documents: we send the document list and check the format is acceptable.
  3. Prepare and submit: we complete forms and submit the application.
  4. Biometrics: we help book and prepare for biometrics.
  5. Decision: we track progress and keep you updated until a decision.
  6. After approval: we organise permit copies and set renewal reminders.

Risk & Mitigations

  • Authority timelines: decision times are external; we keep a clean file and track questions to closure.
  • Document standards: legalisation/translation mistakes cause delays; we provide formats and check completeness.
  • Changes after filing: role/salary/address changes may require updates; we advise quickly on next steps.
  • Legal representation: if a lawyer is required, we coordinate them while you deal with MegaServe as the main contact.

FAQs

How long does a General Employment application take?

Preparation is often 2–6 weeks depending on documents and appointments. Decision times depend on the authorities and can change by season.

Can family members be included?

Often yes, depending on the route. We confirm eligibility and prepare the family documents alongside the main application.

Do documents need legalisation and translation?

Often yes. Many foreign documents need legalisation (apostille or equivalent) and certified translation. We confirm what applies to your country.

What if the role or salary changes after filing?

Tell us immediately. Some changes require an update or a new filing. We advise on the cleanest compliant path.

Do you work with immigration lawyers and attend appointments?

Yes. We can coordinate immigration lawyers and, where venue policy allows, provide a MegaServe representative to accompany you.
