Who It’s For

  • Cyprus employers hiring non-EU staff who need predictable checklists, filings, and tracking
  • International groups transferring staff into Cyprus with clean documentation and appointment sequencing
  • Candidates and families who want clear onboarding steps and renewal/change tracking

What You Get (Deliverables)

  • Route scoping note and tailored checklist (employee & employer)
  • Application forms, booking confirmations, and fee schedule
  • Submission proof, reference numbers, and liaison log
  • Approval pack and arrival Day-1/onboarding checklist
  • Change/renewal tracker with reminders and evidence index
  • Structured VDR index and permissions register (audit trail)

Process & Typical Timeline

  1. Scope & Readiness: Confirm route, role, timelines, and dependencies. Issue checklists and templates.
  2. Collect & Validate: Completeness/quality check; certification/legalisation routing; booking prep.
  3. Draft & Submit: Populate forms, assemble pack, file, and log reference numbers.
  4. Liaise & Clarify: Respond to authority queries; submit any additional evidence.
  5. Decision & Appointment Execution: Issue approvals, schedule biometrics/registrations, and — where permitted — a MegaServe representative can attend with you for the Migration appointment.
  6. Post-Issue & Renew: Track renewals/changes; maintain index and access register in VDR.

Risk & Mitigations

  • Authority queues: processing times are controlled externally; we sequence tasks and keep status clear.
  • EDD / additional checks: may extend timelines; we prepare supporting evidence up front and log clarifications.
  • Document quality: certifications/legalisations must meet standards; we provide formats to reduce resubmissions.
  • Change events: role, salary, address changes may trigger updates; we track and advise on required steps.

FAQs

Informational only—this is not legal or immigration advice. Final positions rest with licensed advisors and competent authorities.

Do you handle the process directly or through immigration lawyers?

Both. MegaServe coordinates the end-to-end process and is your single point of contact. Depending on the case, we either (i) coordinate with immigration lawyers for legal components, and/or (ii) provide MegaServe representatives to manage the execution and attend Migration appointments with you where permitted.

How long does the permit process take?

Timelines depend on route, authority queues, and document readiness. We set gates, keep the evidence file clean, and update you on milestones.

Can family members be included?

Often yes, subject to route rules and evidence. We sequence dependants to match the principal application and approvals.

Do you guarantee approvals?

No. We present complete, well-evidenced applications and maintain a clear audit trail to reduce queries and delays.

What if details change after filing?

We log changes (role, address, employer notes), notify the authority as required, and update the renewal tracker.
