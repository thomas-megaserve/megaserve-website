Employment Permit for Non-E.U. Nationals

We check if you qualify, prepare the documents, submit the application, book biometrics, and track it until a decision.

Who It’s For

  • Cyprus employers hiring non-EU staff who want clear steps and predictable handling
  • International groups transferring staff to Cyprus and needing clean documents and appointment planning
  • Candidates (and families) who want clear next steps and renewal reminders

What You Get

  • Route confirmation (what route, and why)
  • A clear document list for the employer, employee, and (if needed) family
  • Prepared forms and a submission record (what was filed and when)
  • Biometrics booking support and attendance notes
  • Permit copies and renewal reminders
  • Clean records for banks, auditors, and future renewals

Process & Typical Timeline

  1. Route check: we confirm the right route and any risks.
  2. Collect documents: we send the document list and check the format is acceptable.
  3. Prepare and submit: we complete forms and submit the application.
  4. Biometrics: we help book and prepare for biometrics.
  5. Decision: we track progress and keep you updated until a decision.
  6. After approval: we organise permit copies and set renewal reminders.

Risk & Mitigations

  • Authority queues: timelines depend on the authorities; we plan steps early and track progress.
  • Extra checks: some cases need extra information; we prepare supporting documents early.
  • Document standards: incorrect or missing documents cause delays; we provide formats and check for completeness.
  • Changes after filing: role/salary/address changes can require updates; we advise quickly on next steps.

FAQs

Informational only — this is not legal or immigration advice. Final decisions rest with the competent authorities.

Do you work directly or through immigration lawyers?

Both. MegaServe manages the process and remains your single point of contact. If a lawyer is required, we coordinate them for you.

How long does the process take?

It depends on the route, document readiness, and authority workload. We share a realistic timeline during intake and keep you updated.

Can family members be included?

Often yes, depending on the route. We confirm eligibility and prepare the family documents alongside the main application.

Do you guarantee approvals?

No. We prepare a complete, well-organised application and track questions to reduce delays, but the final decision is not ours.

What if details change after filing?

Tell us immediately. Some changes require an update or a new filing. We advise on the cleanest compliant path.
