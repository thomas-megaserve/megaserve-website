Process & Cadence

  1. Intake & Triage: capture role, salary, nationality, family; confirm the correct route and risks.
  2. Employer Setup: register/verify company where needed; draft employer letters and undertakings.
  3. Evidence Pack: compile forms, translations, insurance, police certs; book appointments.
  4. Submission & Biometrics: lodge application, attend biometrics, track reference numbers.
  5. Decision & Issuance: collect permits/cards; issue employment start notes and renewal reminders.

FAQ

How long does an employment permit usually take?
It varies by route and season. After a complete pack and appointment, decisions often run from a few weeks to several months. We share current local timelines during intake.
Can family join on my permit?
Often yes, subject to income, accommodation, and route rules. We confirm eligibility and prepare the dependant pack alongside your application.
Do you handle translations and apostilles?
Yes. We coordinate certified translations and legalisations where required, and confirm when originals will be checked at biometrics.
What if my role or salary changes?
Notify us. Some changes require an update or a new filing. We track trigger events and advise on steps and timing.
Will you attend appointments?
We arrange bookings and provide attendance notes. In-person accompaniment depends on venue policy; where allowed, we can attend or have a local representative meet you.
