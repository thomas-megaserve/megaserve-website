Who It’s For

  • Non-EU founders applying for the Cyprus Start-Up Visa
  • Small teams applying together (with clear roles)
  • Applicants who want one point of contact for documents, submissions, and follow-ups

What You Get

  • Eligibility check (route fit, founder/team roles, and basic requirements)
  • Clear checklist and evidence tracker
  • Prepared submission pack and correspondence log
  • Appointment coordination (if required by the process)
  • Renewal reminders and organised records

Process

  1. Check eligibility: confirm founder/team roles and route fit.
  2. Collect documents: pitch materials, funding proof, IDs, and compliance documents.
  3. Prepare & submit: build the pack and submit it in the required format.
  4. Follow up: respond to authority questions and clarification requests.
  5. Decision: we confirm the result and the next steps.

  • Authority discretion: approval is decided by the authority.
  • Unclear packs: unclear documents cause delays; we keep the file clean and structured.

  • Sequencing: we help prepare next steps early to avoid delays after approval.

FAQs

Informational only — this is not legal or immigration advice. Final decisions rest with competent authorities.

Can I apply as an individual founder?

Yes. Individual founders and small teams may apply. Requirements are confirmed during intake.

Do I need a Cyprus company before applying?

Not always. The sequence depends on the authority workflow. We confirm the best order for your case.

Do you provide legal advice?

No. We coordinate the process and documents. If legal input is required, we coordinate with specialist lawyers.

What if the start-up plan changes?

Renewals depend on programme requirements and compliance. If the plan changes materially, we can review alternative routes.

Can dependants join?

Often yes, depending on the rules. We confirm eligibility and coordinate dependant documents.
