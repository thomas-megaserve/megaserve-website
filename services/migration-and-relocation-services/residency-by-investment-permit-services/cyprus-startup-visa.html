Cyprus Start-Up Visa

A targeted program enabling innovative founders and qualified teams to establish and scale tech or R&D-driven ventures in Cyprus. MegaServe coordinates eligibility scoping, business plan build, submission, and authority liaison until permit issuance.

Overview

The Cyprus Start-Up Visa supports entrepreneurs from non-EU countries wishing to create and operate innovative businesses in Cyprus. The scheme accepts individual founders and teams, granting residence and work rights conditioned on innovation potential and funding capacity.

  • Eligibility evaluation for individual or team application
  • Innovation authority approval preparation
  • Business plan drafting and financial model validation
  • Application assembly, submission, and follow-up
  • Post-approval residence management and renewal calendar

Eligibility & Evidence Matrix (Guide)

Founder Profile Non-EU; age 18+; relevant track record CVs, references, portfolio Team applications allowed

Matrix is indicative; precise criteria and documents are validated during scoping.

Process

  1. Scoping — evaluate concept, team, and route; confirm capital and timelines.
  2. Document build — pitch deck, plan & model, evidence pack, translations/legalisations.
  3. Innovation approval — submit to the authority; manage queries.
  4. Immigration filing — residence/work permits; appointment coordination.
  5. Issuance & onboarding — cards, TIC/TIN, banking and practical setup.
  6. Monitoring & renewal — KPI tracking, reports, and extension preparation.

FAQ

Can I apply as an individual founder?
Yes. The Start-Up Visa accepts both individual and team applications (commonly up to five members). We confirm current limits at scoping.
Do I need a Cyprus company before applying?
No. Incorporation can follow Innovation Authority approval; we handle set-up and banking coordination.
What if the start-up underperforms?
Renewal depends on meeting program KPIs. If operations cease, we can assess alternative visa routes.
Is there a pathway to long-term residence?
Continuous lawful residence may count toward long-term residence eligibility under applicable rules.
Can dependants join?
Yes, spouse/partner and minor children can generally join on linked routes. We coordinate their packs and appointments.
