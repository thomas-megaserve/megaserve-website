Who It’s For

  • Non-EU founders building innovation-led businesses who want clean, authority-ready documentation
  • Teams (commonly up to a small group) applying together with defined roles and funding evidence
  • Applicants who want a single point of contact coordinating company setup, compliance, and migration steps

What You Get (Deliverables)

  • Eligibility scoping memo (route fit, team structure, funding evidence checklist)
  • Structured checklist and evidence tracker (founder/team + business + funding)
  • Submission-ready documentation pack and correspondence log
  • Appointment coordination and post-approval filing support (where applicable)
  • Renewal calendar and secure repository of all filings and evidence

Process & Typical Timeline

  1. Discovery: confirm route fit, founder/team roles, funding capacity, and timing.
  2. Checklist: gather pitch/business documents, funding evidence, IDs, insurance, and civil-status documents.
  3. Pack Build: compile and QA the full submission file; coordinate translations/legalisations where required.
  4. Submission & Liaison: submit and manage follow-ups and clarification requests.
  5. Immigration Steps: coordinate the residence filing steps, appointments, and issuance workflow as applicable.
  6. Post-Issue: keep renewal reminders and maintain an audit-ready record set.

Risk & Mitigations

  • Authority discretion: approval depends on the competent authority; we reduce friction by ensuring a coherent, complete file.
  • Weak documentation: many delays come from unclear packs; we standardise structure and track gaps to closure.
  • Translations/legalisation: we route properly to avoid rejection for format issues.
  • Sequencing: we prepare downstream steps early (company/banking/admin) to prevent post-approval bottlenecks.

FAQs

Informational only — this is not legal or immigration advice. Final decisions rest with competent authorities.

Can I apply as an individual founder?

Yes. The Start-Up Visa can accept individual founders and also small teams. We confirm the current structure and requirements during intake.

Do I need a Cyprus company before applying?

Not always. Incorporation can be sequenced based on the route and authority workflow. We coordinate timing and documentation to avoid rework.

Do you provide legal advice?

No. We coordinate the process and documentation. Where legal input is required, we coordinate with specialist lawyers. We can also provide our own representatives to accompany clients to appointments where permitted.

What if the start-up underperforms?

Renewals depend on the program’s requirements and continued compliance. If the plan changes materially, we can assess alternative residence routes.

Can dependants join?

Often yes, subject to route rules and evidence. We confirm eligibility at scoping and coordinate dependant documentation.
