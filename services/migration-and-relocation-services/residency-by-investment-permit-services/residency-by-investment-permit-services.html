  2. Evidence Build: compile source-of-funds, property/shareholding docs, translations/apostilles.

FAQs

How long does Regulation 6.2 take?

Timelines vary by office load and completeness. After a complete pack and appointment, decisions often run from weeks to a few months. We share current local estimates during intake.

Can family members be included?

Usually yes, subject to income, accommodation, and route rules. We confirm eligibility and prepare the dependant pack alongside your application.

What counts as acceptable source of funds?

Salary, business income, savings, or sale proceeds with documentary evidence. We compile a clear provenance trail with bank letters and statements.

Do you assist with translations and apostilles?

Yes. We coordinate certified translations and legalisations and confirm when originals will be checked at appointments.

If my investment changes after filing?

Some changes require updates or re-filing. We advise on implications and next steps.
