Permanent Residence (Reg. 6.2) — Accelerated

Who It’s For

  • Non-EU nationals applying for Cyprus Permanent Residence under Reg. 6.2
  • Applicants who want a clear source of funds file and clean records
  • Families that need linked packs and renewal/conditions tracking

What You Get

  • Eligibility confirmation (investment route, income, and family scope)
  • Source of funds file (bank letters, statements, and clear notes)
  • Investment/property evidence pack (agreements, receipts, confirmations)
  • Submission receipts, reference numbers, and appointment coordination
  • Status updates and a clear questions/answers log (if requested by the authorities)
  • Secure copies of permits and a conditions/renewals diary

Process & Typical Timeline

  1. Check eligibility: confirm investment route, income, family members, and target dates.
  2. Collect documents: we confirm what needs translation/legalisation.
  3. Prepare & submit: forms and supporting evidence, including source of funds.
  4. Follow up: respond to authority questions and track status.
  5. Approval: collect the permit and store secure copies.
  6. After approval: track the programme conditions and renewal reminders.

Eligibility Guide (Simple)

Factor What matters Typical evidence Notes
Investment Qualifying property or approved investment Sale agreement, receipts, title/assignment, confirmations Accepted options confirmed at scoping
Income Stable income meeting the programme rules Bank statements, tax returns, payslips, dividends Threshold depends on household size
Source of funds Clear, lawful funds trail Bank trails, contracts, sale proceeds evidence Extra checks can apply
Accommodation & insurance Address proof and medical cover Lease/title; insurance policy Family can change insurance scope
Clean record Valid passport and police certificate Passport copy; police certificate Translation/legalisation may be needed

This is a guide only. Final requirements are confirmed case-by-case and by the competent authorities.

Risk & Practical Points

  • Source of funds clarity: we structure the trail so it is easy to review.
  • Translations/legalisation: we confirm what is required and route it correctly.
  • Authority queues: timelines depend on authority workload; we keep tracking and status clear.
  • Conditions: we help you track conditions (investment holding, insurance, absences) and renewal reminders.

FAQs

Informational only — this is not legal or immigration advice. Final decisions rest with competent authorities.

What investments qualify under Reg. 6(2)?

Usually qualifying real estate or other permitted investments. We confirm the accepted option and documents during scoping.

How is stable income assessed?

We check your income sources against the programme rules and household size and prepare the supporting evidence.

Do documents need translation or legalisation?

Often yes. We confirm what is needed based on the issuing country and the authority requirements.

Can spouse and children be included?

Often yes, depending on the programme rules. We confirm eligibility and prepare linked family packs.

How long does the accelerated path take?

Preparation depends on property paperwork, source of funds trails, and translations. Decision timing depends on the authorities.

Nothing here is legal advice; requirements are confirmed case-by-case and by the competent authorities.
