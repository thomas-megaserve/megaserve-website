Who It’s For

  • Third-country nationals pursuing Cyprus permanent residence through a qualifying investment path
  • Applicants who want a clean source-of-funds narrative and bankable documentation structure
  • Households that need linked dependant packs and a predictable evidence/renewal trail

What You Get (Deliverables)

  • Eligibility memo covering investment path, income, and household scope
  • Structured source-of-funds file: bank letters, statements, provenance notes
  • Investment/property evidence pack (agreements, receipts, confirmations)
  • Submission receipts, reference numbers, and appointment coordination
  • Status notes and clarification log until decision
  • Secure archive of permits and an ongoing conditions/renewals diary

Process & Typical Timeline

  1. Discovery: confirm investment route, income profile, dependants, and timing window.
  2. Checklist & KYC: gather source-of-funds evidence, civil status documents, insurance, and address proofs.
  3. Build: forms, declarations, translations/legalisations, and evidence compilation.
  4. Submit & Liaise: filing, tracking, and responses to authority queries.
  5. Decision & Issuance: approvals/collections and secure copies for your records.
  6. Post-issue: conditions tracking (investment/coverage/absences) and renewal reminders.

FAQs

Informational only — this is not legal or immigration advice. Final decisions rest with competent authorities.

What investments qualify under Reg. 6(2)?

Typically approved real estate or other permitted investments meeting the current criteria. We confirm acceptable categories and documentation during scoping.

How is stable income assessed?

We map your income streams (salary, dividends, rentals, etc.) against accepted thresholds for household size and compile supporting evidence.

Do documents need legalisation and translation?

Most foreign-issued documents require legalisation/apostille and certified translation. We provide country-specific instructions and templates.

Can spouse and children be included?

Spouse/partner and minor children are typically eligible. Other dependants can be route-specific. We confirm eligibility and build linked packs at scoping.

How long does the accelerated path take?

Preparation commonly takes several weeks depending on property paperwork, source-of-funds trails, and translations. Decision timing is at the discretion of authorities.
