Route Page

Visitors Permit — Requirements & Timeline

The route page contains the detailed checklist, forms, and route-specific processing expectations.

Process & Cadence

  1. Intake: confirm purpose of stay, travel history, and target timeline.
  2. Evidence Pack: compile funds, accommodation, insurance, and translations/legalisations where required.
  3. Submission & Biometrics: lodge application, attend appointment, track references.
  4. Follow-ups: respond to queries and provide additional documents if requested.
  5. Decision & Next Steps: archive permits and set reminders for extensions/renewals.

How This Connects to Other Services

  • Accommodation: coordination for tenancy documents and practical setup (where needed)
  • Banking: letters and documentation support based on permit status (where relevant)
  • Corporate services: support for clients who combine residence with company setup and administration

FAQs

Is this the detailed visitor route page?

No — this is the hub page. The detailed route page is linked above and contains the full checklist, forms, and route-specific processing expectations.

Can you coordinate appointments and accompany clients?

We coordinate booking and provide attendance notes. Where the venue allows, we can attend with the client or provide a local representative.

Do you work with lawyers?

Yes. We coordinate with immigration lawyers where legal input is required, and we also provide our own representatives to accompany clients to Migration where permitted—so the client can have a single point of contact through MegaServe.

What if I need an extension?

We track expiry dates and assemble the extension pack in advance. Updated funds/insurance and any change notes are typically required.

Do you handle translations and apostilles?

Yes — certified translations and legalisations are coordinated where required, with guidance on originals needed at biometrics.
