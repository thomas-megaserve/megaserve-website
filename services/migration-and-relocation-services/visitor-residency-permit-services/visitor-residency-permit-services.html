Visitor Residency Permit Services

Long-stay “Visitor” permits and extensions arranged end-to-end—eligibility, evidence, appointments, and follow-ups until decision.

What We Handle

  • Eligibility triage (nationality, purpose, stay length, prior entries)
  • Evidence build: financial sufficiency, accommodation, insurance
  • Forms, translations/apostilles, certified copies as required
  • Appointment booking, biometrics preparation, attendance notes
  • Submission handling and liaison on authority clarifications
  • Extensions, re-entry planning, and renewal diary
  • Status letters for banks, landlords, and schools (where relevant)
  • Secure archive of receipts, reference numbers, and permits

Process & Cadence

  1. Intake: confirm purpose of stay, travel history, and target timeline.
  2. Evidence Pack: compile funds, accommodation, insurance, and translations/apostilles.
  3. Submission & Biometrics: lodge application, attend appointment, track references.
  4. Follow-ups: respond to queries, provide additional documents if requested.
  5. Decision & Extension Plan: archive permits and set reminders for extensions/next steps.

FAQ

How long does a Visitor permit take?
Timing depends on office load and completeness. After a complete pack and appointment, decisions often run from several weeks to a few months. We provide current local estimates during intake.
Can I work on a Visitor permit?
No. Visitor routes do not grant work rights. If employment is intended, we will triage an appropriate employment-based route.
Can my family be included?
Often yes, subject to evidence of funds, accommodation, insurance, and relationship proofs. We prepare dependant packs alongside the principal filing.
What if I need an extension?
We track expiry dates and assemble the extension pack in advance. Updated funds/insurance and any change notes are typically required.
Do you handle translations and apostilles?
Yes—certified translations and legalisations are coordinated where required, with guidance on originals needed at biometrics.
