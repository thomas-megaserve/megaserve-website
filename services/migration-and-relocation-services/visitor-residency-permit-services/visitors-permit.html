Visitors Permit

For non-EU nationals who wish to remain in Cyprus without employment. We scope eligibility, build the pack, file, liaise with authorities, and manage renewals for individuals and families.

Cyprus-based coordination · Clear evidence packs · Renewal tracking and structured archive

Note: A Visitors Permit does not allow work in Cyprus. If employment is intended, see Employment Permit Services.

Who it’s for What you get Process Case study Requirements Risk FAQs Start

Who It’s For

  • Retirees or self-sufficient individuals with income from abroad who want to reside in Cyprus without employment
  • Families who need a predictable checklist and appointment coordination
  • Applicants who want renewal tracking and clean records (to avoid last-minute scrambles)

What You Get (Deliverables)

  • Eligibility confirmation and tailored checklist (principal + dependants if applicable)
  • Application pack preparation, submission receipts, and a correspondence log
  • Appointment booking coordination and attendance notes
  • Document handling: certified translations/legalisations where required
  • Renewal diary with reminders and evidence refresh tracking
  • Secure archive of filings and issued permits

Process & Typical Timeline

  1. Scoping: confirm purpose of stay, household, and route fit (no employment).
  2. Evidence Pack: compile funds, accommodation, insurance, and civil status documents.
  3. Build & File: complete forms, assemble the pack, submit, and log reference numbers.
  4. Appointments: book biometrics/attendance steps and prepare the client for the visit.
  5. Follow-ups: respond to authority queries and supply additional evidence if requested.
  6. Decision & Renewal Plan: secure copies, archive, and set renewal reminders.

Risk & Mitigations

  • Employment intent: visitors route does not allow work; we triage alternative routes if employment is intended.
  • Evidence clarity: we structure funds and insurance evidence to reduce authority queries and delays.
  • Timing: authority appointment availability varies; we book early and keep a weekly status cadence.
  • Renewals: we maintain a diary and refresh evidence in advance to prevent expiry-window stress.

FAQs

Can I work while holding a Visitors Permit?

No. Employment or business activity in Cyprus is not permitted under this permit.

How long is the Visitors Permit valid?

Commonly issued up to 1 year and renewable if conditions continue to be met (final duration depends on current practice and your file).

Do you work with lawyers?

Yes. We coordinate with immigration lawyers where legal input is required, and we also provide our own representatives to accompany clients to Migration where permitted—so the client can have a single point of contact through MegaServe.

What if I need an extension or renewal?

We track expiry dates and assemble the renewal pack in advance. Updated funds/insurance and any change notes are typically required.

Do you handle translations and apostilles?

Yes — certified translations and legalisations are coordinated where required, with guidance on originals needed at biometrics.
