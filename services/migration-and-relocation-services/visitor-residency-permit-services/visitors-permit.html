Visitors Permit

For non-EU nationals who want to live in Cyprus without working. We check eligibility, prepare the documents, submit the application, and help with renewals.

Cyprus-based coordination · Clear steps · Clear tracking
Email Us Start Now

Note: A Visitors Permit does not allow work in Cyprus. If you plan to work, see Employment Permit Services.

Who it’s for What you get Process Case study Requirements Risk FAQs Start

Who It’s For

  • Retirees or self-sufficient applicants with income from abroad
  • Families who want a clear checklist and appointment support
  • Applicants who want organised records for renewals

What You Get

  • Eligibility confirmation and a clear checklist (main applicant + family, if any)
  • Prepared application pack and submission receipts
  • Appointment booking support and attendance notes
  • Translations and legalisation arranged where required
  • Renewal reminders and organised records
  • Secure archive of filings and issued permits

Process & Typical Timeline

  1. Check eligibility: confirm purpose of stay and household (no employment).
  2. Collect documents: funds, accommodation, insurance, civil status.
  3. Prepare & submit: forms and supporting documents.
  4. Appointments: book biometrics/attendance steps and prepare you for the visit.
  5. Follow up: respond to authority questions and provide extra documents if needed.
  6. Decision & renewals: archive the permit and set reminders.

Eligibility & Evidence Matrix

  • No employment: this route does not allow work. We can suggest the correct route if work is planned.

  • Renewals: we track expiry dates and refresh evidence in advance.

FAQs

Informational only — this is not legal or immigration advice. Final decisions rest with competent authorities.

Can I work while holding a Visitors Permit?

No. Work or business activity in Cyprus is not allowed under this permit.

How long is the Visitors Permit valid?

Often up to 1 year and renewable if conditions continue to be met (final duration depends on the authorities and your file).

Do you work with lawyers?

Yes, where legal input is needed. We coordinate through MegaServe as your single point of contact.

What if I need an extension or renewal?

We track expiry dates and prepare the renewal pack in advance. Updated funds and insurance are usually required.

Do you handle translations and apostilles?

Yes. We coordinate certified translations and legalisations where required.
