Overview

Most delays happen when the wrong route is chosen, documents are missing, or appointments are booked in the wrong order. We keep the process organised and keep clear records of what was filed and when.

Who It’s For

  • Employers hiring staff into Cyprus and needing permit coordination
  • Individuals relocating for work, investment, or long-stay residence
  • Families joining a main applicant with linked permits and appointments
  • Applicants who want one point of contact (including lawyer coordination where required)

How We Work

  1. Route check: confirm the correct route and key requirements.
  2. Checklist: send a clear checklist and templates; confirm translations/legalisation if needed.
  3. Appointments: book and coordinate appointments (main applicant + family members).
  4. Submission: submit the file, track reference numbers, and log authority questions to closure.
  5. After approval: help with next steps (if applicable) and set renewal reminders.

Where legal representation is required, we coordinate immigration lawyers—while you still deal with MegaServe as your single point of contact. Where venue policy allows, a MegaServe representative can accompany clients to Migration appointments.

FAQs

Do you provide immigration legal advice?

No. We provide practical coordination, document packs, bookings, and process management. Where legal advice is required, we coordinate with immigration lawyers.

Can you handle family members and dependants?

Yes. We prepare and book family packs and appointments alongside the main applicant and track linked renewals where applicable.

How long do Cyprus permits take?

Processing times vary by route and office workload. We share current local expectations during intake and keep status notes until decision.

Do you attend appointments?

We coordinate bookings and provide attendance notes. Where venue policy allows, we can provide a MegaServe representative to accompany clients to Migration appointments.
