Who It’s For

  • Foreign spouses of Cypriot citizens who want an eligibility memo before filing
  • Couples with foreign-issued civil records (apostille/legalisation + translation needed)
  • Applicants who want structured evidence and predictable follow-ups until decision

What You Get

  • Eligibility memo covering marriage duration and cohabitation evidence
  • Checklist + templates, with translation/legalisation routing where needed
  • Compiled filing pack and submission receipts/reference tracking
  • Authority liaison and a structured log of clarifications
  • Oath/registration step plan and post-approval handover notes
  • Secure, organised archive of evidence for future use

Process

  1. Scoping: confirm route assumptions, marriage timeline, and household details.
  2. Evidence build: marriage certificate, cohabitation proofs, civil status, police certificates, translations/legalisations.
  3. Build & file: complete forms, assemble the pack, submit, and log reference numbers.
  4. Follow-ups: respond to authority queries and supply additional evidence if requested.
  5. Decision & oath: coordinate post-approval steps and provide a clear handover plan.

Risk & Mitigations

  • Evidence gaps: we structure cohabitation evidence so the timeline is continuous and readable.
  • Record inconsistency: we align names, dates, and civil records to avoid contradictions.
  • Document handling: we route legalisation/translation correctly to reduce resubmission risk.
  • Authority timing: decision timing is controlled externally; we keep status clear and track queries to closure.

FAQs

Do we need to prove cohabitation?

Yes, typically via joint leases/titles, utilities, and other shared records covering the qualifying period.

Which documents need legalisation or translation?

Foreign-issued civil records and police certificates commonly require apostille/legalisation and certified translation.

Do you work with lawyers and accompany clients?

Yes. We coordinate with immigration lawyers where legal input is required, and we can also provide our own representatives to accompany clients to appointments where permitted—so the client can keep MegaServe as the single point of contact.

Is an interview required?

Some cases require interview. We prepare you with document cross-checks and typical question frameworks.

How long do decisions take?

Timelines depend on office load and dossier completeness. We provide realistic expectations during intake and track the file through to decision.
