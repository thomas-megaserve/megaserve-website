Process

  1. Intake & timeline: confirm the route, residence years, and travel gaps.
  2. Collect documents: police certificates, civil status documents, residence proof, and other evidence.
  3. Prepare & submit: forms, fees, and the full pack.
  4. Follow up: respond to requests for more documents.
  5. Decision & next steps: approvals and any final steps (oath/registration) where required.

Lawyers and In-Person Support

  • Lawyer coordination: if legal input is required, we coordinate with immigration lawyers and keep the file organised.
  • Our representatives: where the venue allows, we can attend appointments with you or send a representative.

FAQs

How is eligibility assessed?

We review residence years, travel gaps, language readiness (if required), and good character, then confirm the correct route.

Can you help before I qualify?

Yes. We can plan the timeline, track renewals, and keep your records organised so the final file is easier to compile.

Do you work with lawyers and attend appointments?

Yes. We coordinate with immigration lawyers when needed. Where the venue allows, we can attend with you or provide a representative.

Do you handle translations and legalisations?

Yes. We coordinate certified translations and legalisation where required, and track them in the evidence log.

How long do decisions take?

It depends on authority workload and how complete the file is. We share current local expectations at intake and provide monthly updates after submission.
