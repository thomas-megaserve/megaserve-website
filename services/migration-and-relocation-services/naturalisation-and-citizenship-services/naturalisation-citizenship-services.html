Process & Cadence

  1. Intake & Timeline Plan: confirm qualifying residence, travel gaps, and readiness for the chosen route.
  2. Evidence Build: gather police certificates, civil status documents, residence proofs, and language evidence (where required).
  3. Submission & Booking: complete forms/fees, secure appointments, and log reference numbers.
  4. Follow-ups: respond to authority queries and provide supplemental evidence as required.
  5. Decision & Next Steps: approvals, oath/registration steps where applicable, and handover notes for passports/IDs.

How We Work with Lawyers and In-Person Representation

  • Lawyer coordination: where legal input is required, we work with immigration lawyers and coordinate the documentation and filing schedule.
  • Our representatives: where the venue allows, we can accompany clients to appointments through our own representatives, so the client has a single point of contact through MegaServe.

FAQs

How is eligibility for naturalisation assessed?

We review lawful residence years, travel gaps, language readiness (where required), and good character. An eligibility memo sets out the route and any steps before filing.

Can you help while I am still building residence years?

Yes. We can map the timeline, track renewals, and keep the record clean so the eventual citizenship file is easier to compile.

Do you work with lawyers and accompany clients?

Yes. We coordinate with immigration lawyers where legal input is required, and we can also provide our own representatives to accompany clients to appointments where permitted—so the client can keep MegaServe as the single point of contact.

Do you handle translations and legalisations?

Yes — certified translations and legalisations are coordinated and tracked in the evidence log.

How long do decisions take?

Timelines depend on office load and dossier completeness. We share current local expectations at intake and provide monthly status updates after submission.
