Permanent Residence — Categories A–F

Traditional permanent residence pathways for investors, professionals, and self-sufficient applicants under Categories A–F. We scope eligibility, build the filing pack, and manage each stage with full compliance documentation.

Overview

Categories A–F provide alternative residence routes for third-country nationals engaging in business, agriculture, or self-sufficient living in Cyprus. Each category reflects a different economic activity or income base. MegaServe manages the route mapping, evidence preparation, authority filings, and post-approval compliance tracking.

  • Eligibility assessment for all Categories A–F
  • Investment and income verification
  • Checklist and document preparation
  • Application filing and liaison with authorities
  • Renewal reminders and compliance updates

Category Breakdown

Category Applicant Type Key Requirements Notes
A Self-employed in agriculture or animal husbandry Land ownership or long lease; adequate capital and income Must show viability and lawful activity
B Self-employed in mining enterprises Valid mining license; sufficient capital and experience Rarely used; regulatory approval needed
C Self-employed in trade or profession Investment of capital in business in Cyprus Requires business plan and financial proof
D Self-employed in profession or science Professional qualifications; income and business premises Subject to local licensing
E Employment offer in Cyprus Secure job; employer commitment and income stability Company must be established in Cyprus
F Self-sufficient (no work in Cyprus) Stable income and/or funds abroad, accommodation proof Most used among retirees and remote earners

This matrix is indicative; criteria may vary based on the applicant’s activity and documentation readiness.

FAQ

What is the difference between Reg. 6(2) and Categories A–F?
Reg. 6(2) is the accelerated permanent residence based on investment. Categories A–F are traditional routes based on economic activity, employment, or self-sufficiency.
Which category is most common?
Category F is the most frequently used, typically for self-sufficient applicants or retirees with stable income from abroad.
Can dependants be included?
Spouses and minor children are generally eligible for inclusion, subject to income and accommodation criteria.
How long does it take to process?
Preparation takes 4–8 weeks, while authority decision times vary depending on volume and documentation quality.
Is work permitted under Category F?
No, Category F is for self-sufficient individuals who do not intend to work in Cyprus. Other categories allow economic activity as defined.

This information is a practical summary. Requirements may change with regulation updates.
