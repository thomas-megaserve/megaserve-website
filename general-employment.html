General Employment — Cyprus

Fast, compliant onboarding for standard roles. We scope eligibility, prepare filings, liaise with authorities, and coordinate arrival onboarding with audit-ready records.

Overview

For hires that do not fall under “highly-skilled” or EU Blue Card, the General Employment route is the practical pathway. MegaServe manages each stage end-to-end and keeps your compliance posture clean.

  • Route & salary-band scoping against current practice
  • Jurisdiction-specific checklists, templates, and translation guidance
  • Application build, filing, and authority liaison
  • Dependants add-ons (where eligible)
  • Arrival onboarding: Social Insurance, TIC/TIN, payroll/banking coordination
  • Renewal calendar and audit-ready repository

Process

  1. Discovery & role mapping — confirm route, sponsor readiness, timing.
  2. Checklist — gather documents; flag items for legalisation/translation.
  3. Application build — forms, evidence, employer attestations.
  4. Submission & liaison — file, track, and handle authority queries.
  5. Decision & collections — approvals, biometrics/permits as required.
  6. Onboarding — Social Insurance, TIC/TIN, payroll & banking coordination.
  7. Renewals — reminders and audit trail maintenance.

FAQ

How long does a General Employment application take?
Preparation is typically 2–6 weeks depending on document readiness. Decision times are set by authorities and vary by season and workload.
Can family members be included?
In many cases, dependants can apply on linked routes. We confirm eligibility at scoping and coordinate their documentation and filings.
Do documents need to be apostilled or legalised?
Yes, many foreign-issued documents require apostille or equivalent legalisation, plus certified translation. We provide jurisdiction-specific guidance.
What if the role or salary changes after filing?
Material changes may require an update or re-filing. Notify us immediately so we can advise on the cleanest compliant path.
Can MegaServe assist with Social Insurance and TIC/TIN?
Yes. Our Complete level includes arrival onboarding for Social Insurance and TIC/TIN, with payroll and banking coordination where requested.
