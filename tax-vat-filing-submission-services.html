Tax & VAT Filing / Submission Services

Accurate, timely filings with clean audit trails—so banks, auditors, and authorities are comfortable and your team has clarity every period.

What We Handle

  • Periodic VAT returns and VIES recapitulative statements
  • Intrastat coordination where thresholds apply (with your accountants)
  • Provisional/estimated tax coordination and annual compliance support
  • e-Services portal submissions & payment instructions/reference notes
  • Reconciliations: sales/purchase ledgers ↔ bank ↔ VAT control accounts
  • Authority liaison: clarifications, queries, and verifications
  • Rolling compliance calendar with reminders and status visibility

Process & Cadence

  1. Intake: ledger exports, bank statements, adjustments, and supporting docs via secure data room.
  2. Reconcile: cross-checks (ledger↔bank↔VAT control), variance analysis, exceptions list.
  3. Prepare: draft returns, VIES, and any Intrastat data (with your accountants where relevant).
  4. Approve: brief sign-off note highlighting movements and any assumptions.
  5. Submit & Pay: lodge on e-Services; provide payment references and cut-off guidance.
  6. Archive: receipts, working papers, and a period pack stored and indexed for audit.

FAQ

Do you replace our accountants?
No. We handle administration, reconciliations, submissions, and liaison. Tax technical positions remain with your advisors.
Can you help with late or corrective filings?
Yes. We assemble prior-period packs, coordinate adjustments with your accountants, and submit corrections per authority rules.
Will you manage payments?
We provide payment references and timing guidance. Execution remains with your authorised banking users under dual-control.
Can you work across multiple entities?
Yes. We maintain separate calendars and archives, and can share a consolidated dashboard/status note.
