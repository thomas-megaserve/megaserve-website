Residency by Investment Permit Services

Fixed-investment and entrepreneur routes to Cyprus residency with clear evidence, timing, and authority coordination.

What We Handle

  • Route selection: Regulation 6.2, Categories A–F, and Cyprus Start-Up Visa
  • Eligibility checks and funds profiling (assets, income, dependants)
  • Source-of-funds/KYC file: bank letters, statements, provenance notes
  • Property/company evidence: sale agreement, shareholding, valuations
  • Application forms, translations, apostilles, and certified copies

Process & Cadence

  1. Intake & Route Triage: confirm investment path, family scope, and timing windows.
  2. Evidence Build: compile SOF/KYC, property or shareholding docs, translations/apostilles.

  4. Follow-ups: respond to clarifications, update status notes, and manage add-on dependants.
  5. Decision & Archive: collect permits, issue renewal diary, and archive in a secure data room.

FAQ

How long does Regulation 6.2 take?
Timelines vary by office load and completeness. After a complete pack and appointment, decisions often run from weeks to a few months. We share current local estimates during intake.
Can family members be included?
Usually yes, subject to income, accommodation, and route rules. We confirm eligibility and prepare the dependant pack alongside your application.
What counts as acceptable source-of-funds?
Salary, business income, savings, or sale proceeds with documentary evidence. We compile a clear provenance trail with bank letters and statements.
Do you assist with translations and apostilles?
Yes. We coordinate certified translations and legalisations and confirm when originals will be checked at appointments.
If my investment changes after filing?
Some changes require updates or re-filing. We advise on implications and next steps.
