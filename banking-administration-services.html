Banking Administration Services

Open, maintain, and optimise your banking footprint in Cyprus and the EU. We handle selection, onboarding, KYC/KYB, user access, reviews, and ongoing relationship management.

What We Manage

  • Bank/EMI shortlist aligned to activity profile, jurisdictions, and counterparties
  • KYC/KYB dossier: corporate docs, activity narrative, flow mapping, beneficial ownership
  • Account opening: forms, portal uploads, interviews, clarifications, approvals
  • Payment rails: SEPA/SCT Inst, SWIFT, Faster Payments, local corridors where available
  • Merchant acquiring & PSP introductions; gateway settings & MCC alignment
  • User access & controls: signatory matrix, roles, limits, and 2FA policy
  • Ongoing administration: signatory changes, reference letters, statements, attestations
  • Periodic reviews: KYC refresh cycles, alerts, remediation, and status notes

Footprint Options

Channel Typical Use Strengths Considerations
Cyprus / EU Bank Primary operating account; payroll; vendor payments Broad rails; credibility with auditors & counterparties More stringent onboarding; review cycles can be formal
EU EMI (E-money) Multi-currency collections; fast onboarding adjunct Speed; API-friendly; virtual IBANs Limits/features vary; settlement and tiering policies apply
Merchant Acquirer / PSP Card processing and gateway routing Higher approval ratios; risk tools & dashboards MCC fit; chargeback handling; reserve/rolling terms

Final eligibility remains at the discretion of the bank/EMI/acquirer. We position the file to maximise acceptance probability.

Opening Flow

  1. Fit Assessment: activity, jurisdictions, counterparties, projected volumes, and beneficial owners.
  2. Dossier Build: corporate certificates, registers, IDs/PoAs, activity narrative, flow & corridor map.
  3. Submission & Interviews: portal forms, clarifications, compliance interviews, risk notes.
  4. Activation: user setup, signatory matrix, limits, 2FA, test payments, card & FX settings.

Deliverables

  • Banking shortlist with pros/cons, dependencies, and sequence of applications
  • KYC/KYB dossier ready for upload (structured file tree + PDFs)
  • Activity narrative and payment flow diagram for compliance reviews
  • Signatory matrix & user access plan (roles, limits, dual-control options)
  • Merchant/PSP intake pack and gateway settings checklist
  • Banking maintenance calendar (reviews, attestations, card/limit refresh dates)

FAQ

Can you guarantee account opening?
No firm can guarantee approvals. We position the file, sequence applications, and escalate appropriately to maximise acceptance.
Do you work with both banks and EMIs?
Yes. We maintain options across Cyprus/EU banks and reputable EMIs to balance credibility, speed, and feature set.
What if our business has historical issues?
We prepare a candid compliance narrative, address past items directly, and propose clean payment flows and controls.
Can you support card acquiring and PSP setup?
Yes. We prepare merchant packs, map MCCs, and coordinate with PSPs/gateways for a smooth go-live.
