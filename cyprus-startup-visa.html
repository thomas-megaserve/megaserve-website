Cyprus Start-Up Visa

A targeted program enabling innovative founders and qualified teams to establish and scale tech or R&D-driven ventures in Cyprus. MegaServe coordinates eligibility scoping, business plan build, submission, and authority liaison until permit issuance.

Overview

The Cyprus Start-Up Visa supports entrepreneurs from non-EU countries wishing to create and operate innovative businesses in Cyprus. The scheme accepts individual founders and teams, granting residence and work rights conditioned on innovation potential and funding capacity.

  • Eligibility evaluation for individual or team application
  • Innovation authority approval preparation
  • Business plan drafting and financial model validation
  • Application assembly, submission, and follow-up
  • Post-approval residence and renewal management

Key Eligibility Criteria

  • Non-EU national over 18 years of age
  • Innovative business concept with potential for growth and export
  • Minimum capital requirement — €20,000 (subject to change)
  • Proof of sufficient funds to cover living expenses
  • Comprehensive business plan and CVs of founders

Process

  1. Scoping call — evaluate business concept, structure, and eligibility.
  2. Document & plan preparation — compile pitch deck, financial projections, and application forms.
  3. Submission to Innovation Authority — receive approval for innovative status.
  4. Immigration filing — residence and work permit application lodged.
  5. Permit issuance — founder and team receive Cyprus residence cards.
  6. Monitoring & renewal — performance reporting and extension assistance.

FAQ

Can I apply as an individual founder?
Yes. The Start-Up Visa accepts both individual and team applications. Teams may include up to five founders.
Do I need to have a company incorporated before applying?
No, the company can be incorporated after Innovation Authority approval, with assistance from MegaServe.
What happens if the start-up fails?
Permit renewal depends on performance criteria. If the business ceases, residence status may expire unless transitioned to another permit.
Is there a path to permanent residence?
Continuous residence for five years may qualify you for long-term residence under EU Directive 2003/109/EC.
Can dependants join the founder?
Yes, spouse and minor children may apply for residence under family reunification rules after initial approval.

Information subject to policy updates by the Cyprus Ministry of Interior and the Research & Innovation Authority.
