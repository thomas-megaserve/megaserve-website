Employment Permit for Non-E.U. Nationals

From sponsor readiness to residence card in hand: eligibility scoping, document packs, filing appointments, biometrics, and onboarding—delivered with clear checklists and timelines.

What We Do

  • Eligibility & route selection (company type, role level, dependants, timelines).
  • Sponsor readiness: company documents, payroll/tax registration, premises proof.
  • Employment contract templates aligned to route requirements.
  • Document pack assembly: IDs, academic/professional proofs, police/medical (if required).
  • Application filing, fee guides, and appointment scheduling.
  • Biometrics & residence card logistics; interim permits where applicable.
  • Family applications (spouse/children) and school/settling notes.
  • Renewals & changes: role updates, employer change, address changes, re-entry.

*Exact evidence and eligibility depend on the selected route and current authority guidance. We align files to prevailing standards.

Process

  1. Scope & Readiness: Confirm route, employer status, role fit, and dependants. Issue tailored checklist.
  2. Collect & Prepare: Assemble documents; certify/legalise where needed; draft employment contract.
  3. File & Appointments: Prepare forms, submit application, schedule biometrics/collection.
  4. Follow-ups: Respond to queries, track status, and manage interim permissions as applicable.
  5. Issue & Onboard: Receive decision/card; register addresses/SS/Tax where required.
  6. Maintain & Renew: Diary key dates; process renewals, changes of role/employer, and dependant updates.

FAQ

Information here is general and not legal or immigration advice. We coordinate with licensed professionals where required.

How long does an employment permit take?
Timelines vary with authority queues and completeness. We scope the route, file quickly once complete, and update you weekly on status.
Can my spouse and children join?
Typically yes, subject to eligibility and evidence (relationship, means, insurance). We prepare and file dependant applications alongside or after the main file.
Do I need to legalise documents?
Some documents require certification, apostille, or legalisation depending on origin and route. We confirm exact requirements in your checklist.
Can I change employer or role later?
Changes are usually possible but may require approvals or re-filing. We assess the impact and manage the process with a documented change note.
Do you guarantee approvals?
No. Decisions rest with the authorities. We reduce risk by aligning evidence to current standards and maintaining complete, audit-ready files.
