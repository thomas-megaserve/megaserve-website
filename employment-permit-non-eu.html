Employment Permit for Non-E.U. Nationals

From eligibility to arrival: role scoping, document prep, filings, authority liaison, and onboarding checklists for non-EU hires in Cyprus—kept tidy, trackable, and audit-ready.

What We Do

  • Eligibility scoping: employer category, role profile, salary bands, and quotas (where applicable).
  • Tailored document checklist for employee & employer; certification/legalisation guidance.
  • Application drafting: forms, appointment scheduling, and fee schedule.
  • Authority liaison: submissions, clarifications, and evidence follow-ups.
  • Family/dependants add-ons and sequencing (if requested).
  • Arrival onboarding: biometric/bookings, social insurance, tax numbers, and bank pack.
  • Renewals & change logs: role changes, address updates, and employer notes.

Eligibility & Evidence Matrix

Category Typical Employer Evidence Employee Evidence Notes
Company Hire (Non-EU) Incorporation set, activity description, payroll/contract draft Passport, CV, degree/certificates, clean record, health insurance Role description & salary aligned to policy
Intra-Group Transfer Group chart, assignment letter, role/tenure proof Passport, employment history, benefits letter Parent/subsidiary relationship documented

Dependants (if any) Employer confirmation (where needed) Passports, marriage/birth certs (certified), cover Sequencing with principal permit

Exact requirements vary by route and authority guidance. We align your file to prevailing expectations and document any exceptions.

Process

  1. Scope & Readiness: Confirm route, role, timelines, and dependencies. Issue checklists and templates.
  2. Collect & Validate: Completeness/quality check; certification/legalisation routing; booking prep.
  3. Draft & Submit: Populate forms, assemble pack, file, and log reference numbers.
  4. Liaise & Clarify: Respond to authority queries; submit any additional evidence.
  5. Decision & Arrival: Issue approvals, schedule biometrics/registrations, and day-1 checklist.
  6. Post-Issue & Renew: Track renewals/changes; maintain index and access register in VDR.

FAQ

Informational only—this is not legal or immigration advice. We coordinate with licensed professionals where required.

Do you replace immigration lawyers?
No. We operate the PMO and documentation. Legal interpretations and representations are handled by licensed counsel; we coordinate and prepare the file.
How long does the permit process take?
Timelines depend on route, authority queues, and document readiness. We set gates, keep a clean evidence file, and update you on milestones.
Can family members be included?
Often yes, subject to route rules and evidence. We sequence dependants to match the principal application and approvals.
Do you guarantee approvals?
No guarantees. We present complete, well-evidenced applications and keep a clear audit trail to reduce queries and delays.
What if details change after filing?
We log changes (role, address, employer notes), notify the authority as required, and update the renewal tracker.
