EU Citizen & Family Members — Registration

Registration and residence documentation for EU citizens and their eligible family members relocating to Cyprus. We scope eligibility, prepare filings, book appointments, and keep every record audit-ready.

Overview

EU citizens exercising free movement in Cyprus, and their eligible family members (EU or non-EU), complete local registration steps for lawful residence and access to services. MegaServe coordinates the evidence pack, submits applications, manages appointments, and handles follow-ups through issuance and renewals.

  • Eligibility scoping for EU citizens and qualifying family members
  • Tailored checklists with templates and translation/legalisation guidance
  • Application build, filing, appointment booking, and authority liaison
  • Linked filings for spouse/partner, children, and dependants
  • Onboarding coordination: TIC/TIN, health, banking (as applicable)
  • Renewal calendar and centralised, audit-ready repository

Registration Routes (Guide)

Route Who It’s For Core Evidence Notes
EU Citizen Registration (Initial) EU/EEA/Swiss nationals residing in Cyprus Passport/ID, employment or self-employment proof, or sufficient resources & insurance, address proof Registration soon after arrival is recommended
Family Member Card (Non-EU) Non-EU family of an EU citizen Passport, evidence of family relationship, EU sponsor registration, co-residence, insurance Legalisation & certified translation commonly required
Permanent Residence (After Qualifying Stay) EU citizens & family after continuous residence period Continuity of residence, employment/resources, address history Absence limits and continuity rules apply

Matrix is indicative; exact documentation, forms, and timing are confirmed at scoping.

Process

  1. Discovery & route mapping — confirm eligibility, dependants, and timing.
  2. Checklist issue — gather core IDs, relationship proofs, employment/resources, insurance, and address documents.
  3. Application build — forms, declarations, and evidence compilation for principal and each dependant.
  4. Submission & appointments — file, schedule, and attend (or accompany) as needed; manage authority queries.
  5. Decision & issuance — collect confirmations/cards and provide secure copies.
  6. Onboarding — TIC/TIN, payroll/benefits alignment, and practical setup where relevant.
  7. Renewals & upgrades — track expiries and switch to permanent status when eligible.

FAQ

Who qualifies as a family member of an EU citizen?
Typically a spouse/registered partner, dependent children, and in some cases dependent parents. We confirm eligibility and evidence at scoping.
Do relationship documents need apostille or legalisation?
Often yes. Foreign-issued certificates usually require apostille/legalisation and certified translation. We provide country-specific guidance.
How long does preparation and decision take?
Preparation is usually 2–6 weeks depending on document readiness and appointment availability. Decision times are set by authorities.
Can non-EU family members work in Cyprus?
Residence cards for qualifying non-EU family members generally permit residence; work permissions depend on the document type and current rules. We advise the correct path during scoping.
When can we apply for permanent residence?
After completing the qualifying continuous residence period and meeting continuity conditions. We track eligibility and prepare the upgrade pack.
