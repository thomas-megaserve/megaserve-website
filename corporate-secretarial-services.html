Corporate Secretarial Services

Company secretary support under Cyprus Companies Law—accurate registers, timely filings, clear minutes, and bank-ready documentation.

What We Do

  • Maintain statutory registers and minute books (digital + physical where required)
  • Prepare and file annual return and routine registrar changes (officers, address, name)
  • Share allotments/transfers, capital increases/reductions, and constitution amendments
  • Issue share certificates, resolutions, powers of attorney, incumbency/good-standing docs
  • UBO/BoR submissions and updates, plus basic TIC/VAT reference updates with advisors
  • Obtain certified copies, apostilles/legalisations, and manage couriering
  • Liaise with auditors, counsel, and banks; provide confirmations and pack documents

Registers We Maintain

  • Directors & secretary
  • Members/shareholders
  • Charges/encumbrances
  • Transfers & allotments
  • UBO/Beneficial Ownership (where applicable)
  • Minute books (board/shareholder)

Share Capital & Ownership Changes – Process

  1. Intake: change description, parties, consents, any SPA/SSA terms.
  2. Drafting: resolutions, notices, share docs, and registrar forms.
  3. Approvals: obtain signatures; hold meeting or circulate written resolutions.
  4. Filing: submit registrar forms; track and confirm acceptance.
  5. Registers & Certificates: update member/transfer registers; issue new certificates.
  6. Notifications: provide updated packs to banks, auditors, and key counterparties.

Authorities & Interfaces

  • Tax/VAT coordination for reference updates with your advisors

  • Auditors and legal counsel (document packs, confirmations)
  • Notaries / Ministry of Justice / MFA for apostilles and legalisations

Documents Checklist (Onboarding)

  • Certificate set, Memorandum & Articles, and latest annual return
  • Current statutory registers and a snapshot of minute books
  • Directors’ and UBO IDs/PoAs; proof of address
  • Any recent registrar filings or draft changes in progress
  • Auditor and bank details; any specific formatting requirements

FAQ

Do you act as company secretary?
Yes. We provide company secretarial services, maintain registers, prepare filings, and keep minutes/resolutions current.
Our registers are incomplete—can you reconstruct them?
Yes. We rebuild from registrar records, historical minutes, and share certificates, then regularise the position with filings.
How fast can you obtain certificates and apostilles?
Typically 1–3 business days, subject to authority queues and courier choice. We provide options and tracking.
Do you handle UBO/BoR submissions?
Yes. We prepare and submit updates and align supporting documents to portal requirements.
Can you coordinate with our auditors and lawyers?
Yes. We manage document packs, confirmations, and timing so third parties receive what they need on schedule.

If you have unusual constraints (legacy cap table, pending disputes, lender covenants), include a brief background when contacting us—we’ll plan accordingly.
