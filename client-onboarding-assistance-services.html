Client Onboarding Assistance Services

Set up intake, verification, and approvals so new clients clear KYC/AML expectations on the first pass—without sacrificing control or auditability.

What We Do

  • Tailored checklists by client type: individual, corporate, trust/foundation, UBOs, signatories.
  • Secure VDR/portal setup with file naming, version control, and permissions.
  • Identity verification workflows: passports/IDs, proof of address, liveness (where applicable).
  • PEP/sanctions/adverse media screening using your approved tools and providers.
  • Ownership & control mapping (cap tables/BO registers) and SoW/SoF narrative templates.
  • Risk assessment methodology and scoring with clear approval notes.
  • Exceptions handling & escalation paths with documented rationale.
  • Bank/account opening packs and periodic KYC refresh calendar.

Process

  1. Scope & Policy Align: Confirm risk appetite, client types, jurisdictions, and evidence thresholds.
  2. Intake & Completeness: Share tailored checklist; set up VDR/portal; perform completeness and quality checks.
  3. Verify & Screen: ID/authenticity checks; PEP/sanctions/adverse media hits recorded with references.
  4. Assess & Approve: Apply risk scoring, draft approval memo, and log decisions/assumptions.
  5. Bank/Account Pack: Assemble forms, resolutions, signatures, and submit via agreed channel.
  6. Archive & Refresh: Index the KYC file, set review dates (e.g., 12/24/36 months), and add to the calendar.

FAQ

This page is informational and not legal or regulatory advice. Final decisions rest with your appointed compliance officer/MLRO, advisors, and the receiving bank.

Do you replace our compliance officer or MLRO?
No. We prepare, organise, and track files, using your policies and approved tools. Approvals and regulatory positions remain with your compliance function.
How long does onboarding usually take?
With complete documents, 2–5 business days is typical. Enhanced due diligence, notarisation/legalisation, or bank clarifications can extend timelines.
Can you prepare files for multiple banks/jurisdictions?
Yes. We tailor checklists and packs to each bank’s format and jurisdictional requirements. We cannot guarantee bank approvals.
How do you handle PEP or sanctions hits?
We document the hit, gather supporting evidence, escalate per policy, and record the decision and rationale. In some cases we may advise not to proceed.
What tools do you work with?
We use your screening/KYC platforms where available, or operate agreed vendor tools. Files are stored in the VDR with role-based access.
