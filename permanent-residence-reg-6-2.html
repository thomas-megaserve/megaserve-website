Permanent Residence (Reg. 6.2) — Accelerated

Fast-track Cyprus PR via qualifying investment and clean-source income. We validate eligibility, coordinate property/investment documentation, file the application, and manage every authority touchpoint through approval.

Overview

Under Regulation 6(2), eligible third-country nationals can obtain permanent residence on an accelerated basis by meeting investment and income criteria. MegaServe delivers end-to-end coordination — from investment evidence and KYC through submission, decision, and onboarding — with an audit-ready trail.

  • Eligibility memo covering investment route, income, and family dependants
  • Property/participation documentation review and source-of-funds pack
  • Complete application build, filing, and authority liaison
  • Dependants handling (spouse/partner, minor children; select adult children where eligible)
  • Onboarding coordination and renewal/conditions tracking
  • Secure repository with immutable evidence archive

Process

  1. Discovery & route mapping — confirm investment option, income, dependants, and timing.
  2. Checklist & KYC — gather SoF, investment contracts, banking trails, and civil status documents.
  3. Application build — forms, declarations, translations/legalisations, and evidence compilation.
  4. Submission & liaison — file, track progress, and manage authority queries.
  5. Decision & issuance — approvals and collection with secure record copies.
  6. Onboarding — local registrations as needed; practical setup and renewals calendar.
  7. Ongoing compliance — monitor conditions (e.g., property/coverage/absences) and update records.

FAQ

What investments qualify under Reg. 6(2)?
Typically approved real estate or other permitted investments meeting the current criteria. We confirm acceptable categories and documentation during scoping.
How is stable income assessed?
We map your income streams (salary, dividends, rentals, etc.) against accepted thresholds for your household size and compile supporting evidence.
Do documents need legalisation and translation?
Yes, most foreign-issued documents require legalisation/apostille and certified translation. We provide country-specific instructions and templates.
Can my spouse and children be included?
Yes. Spouse/partner and minor children are typically eligible; select adult children may qualify subject to conditions. We confirm at scoping and build linked packs.
How long does the accelerated path take?
Preparation usually takes 3–8 weeks depending on property paperwork, SoF trails, and translations. Decision timing is at the discretion of authorities.
