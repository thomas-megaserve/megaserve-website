Visitors Permit

For long-stay visitors, retirees, and family members of residents who wish to remain in Cyprus beyond standard entry durations. MegaServe manages every step — from eligibility scoping and documentation to submission, liaison, and renewal.

Overview

The Visitors Permit allows non-EU nationals to stay in Cyprus for extended periods without engaging in employment. It is commonly used by family members of Cypriot or EU residents, retirees, and individuals with independent means of income. The permit is valid for up to 1 year and renewable.

  • Eligibility confirmation for visitor residency
  • Tailored checklist and document preparation
  • Application filing and tracking with Immigration Department
  • Authority liaison and renewal scheduling
  • Audit-ready record maintenance

Eligibility & Requirements

  • Applicant must not engage in any employment or business in Cyprus
  • Proof of sufficient income from abroad (e.g. pensions, investments, or savings)
  • Valid health insurance policy
  • Rental agreement or proof of accommodation
  • Clean criminal record and valid passport
  • Proof of relationship if applying as family of a resident

Process

  1. Scoping — eligibility confirmation and route selection
  2. Checklist issuance — document list, templates, and timeline
  3. Evidence gathering — income, accommodation, and insurance proofs
  4. Application build — preparation of forms and legalised documents
  5. Submission & liaison — filing, appointment, and tracking
  6. Issuance — collection of residence permit card and renewal scheduling

FAQ

Who can apply for a Visitors Permit?
Any non-EU national who wishes to reside in Cyprus without employment, including retirees or family members of residents.
How long is the Visitors Permit valid?
It is typically issued for up to 1 year and can be renewed annually.
Can I work under a Visitors Permit?
No, employment is not permitted under this permit type.
What if my income is from abroad?
That is acceptable as long as it covers your living expenses in Cyprus.
How long does processing take?
Preparation can take 2–6 weeks, while Immigration Department processing time varies by case.
