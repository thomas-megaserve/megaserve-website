Visitors Permit

For non-EU nationals who wish to remain in Cyprus without employment. We scope eligibility, build the pack, file, liaise with authorities, and manage renewals for individuals and families.

Overview

The Visitors Permit (long-stay visitor) enables extended residence in Cyprus without taking up work. Typical applicants include retirees with income from abroad, family members of residents, and self-sufficient individuals. MegaServe coordinates the end-to-end file with an audit-ready trail.

  • Eligibility memo and route confirmation (solo or family-linked)
  • Checklist, templates, translations/legalisations orchestration
  • Application build, filing, and authority liaison
  • Renewal calendar and evidence refresh tracking
  • Secure repository with immutable evidence archive

Process

  1. Discovery & scoping — confirm eligibility, dependants, and timelines.
  2. Checklist & KYC — compile funds, accommodation, insurance, and civil status docs.
  3. Application build — forms, declarations, translations/legalisations, evidence pack.
  4. Submission & liaison — filing, appointments, and authority queries.
  5. Decision & issuance — card collection and secure copies.
  6. Renewal & monitoring — track expiries and refresh evidence for extensions.

FAQ

Who is eligible for a Visitors Permit?
Non-EU nationals wishing to reside in Cyprus without employment, including retirees and family members of residents, subject to funds, accommodation, and insurance.
How long is the permit valid?
Commonly issued up to 1 year and renewable if conditions continue to be met.
Can I work while holding a Visitors Permit?
No. Employment or business activity in Cyprus is not permitted under this permit.
Is foreign income acceptable?
Yes, provided it sufficiently covers living expenses and is evidenced with bank statements and source documents.
How long does the process take?
Preparation typically takes 2–6 weeks depending on documents and translations; decision times are set by the authorities.
