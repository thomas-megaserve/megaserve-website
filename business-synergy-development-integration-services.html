Business Synergy Development & Integration Services

Identify, prioritise, and realise synergies with a disciplined integration plan—covering operating model, systems, cutover, and measurable benefits.

What We Do

  • Synergy hypothesis framing (revenue, cost, working capital).
  • Operating model design: roles, spans, RACI, decision rights.
  • Process harmonisation: order-to-cash, procure-to-pay, record-to-report, hire-to-retire.
  • Systems & data roadmap: ERP/CRM/MES inventory, interfaces, and migration approach.
  • Cutover planning: rehearsals, go/no-go gates, and contingency paths.
  • Change management: stakeholder map, comms calendar, training packs.
  • Benefits tracking: baselines, KPI definitions, dashboards, and governance.
  • Day-1 & first-90-days execution with risk/issue management (RAID).

Process

  1. Diagnose: Gather baselines, map systems/processes, draft synergy hypotheses, and define guardrails.
  2. Design: Target operating model, workstream charters, and dependency map; confirm KPIs and measurement.
  3. Plan: Integration Master Plan (IMP), RAID and RACI, cutover strategy, training & comms calendar.
  4. Build & Rehearse: Configure processes/systems, data migration approach, and run cutover drills.
  5. Cutover & Day-1: Execute runbook with go/no-go criteria, access & controls, and owner comms.
  6. Stabilise & Track: 30/60/90-day checkpoints, benefit realisation dashboard, and lessons learned.

FAQ

This page is informational and not legal, tax, investment, or engineering advice. We coordinate with licensed professionals and vendors as required.

Do you replace management consultants or IT integrators?
No. We run the integration PMO and synergy governance. Specialist design/build work is performed by your appointed consultants or vendors; we coordinate and quality-control timelines and deliverables.
What’s the typical timeline for a synergy programme?
Diagnostics in 1–3 weeks, planning 2–4 weeks, Day-1 within 4–12 weeks (deal-dependent), and stabilisation over 30/60/90 days. Larger ERP changes extend timelines.
Can you work if both companies use different ERPs/CRMs?
Yes. We map systems, define interim interfaces, and sequence migrations by risk. Vendor lead times and data quality materially impact duration.
Who owns benefits realisation?
Functional owners. We provide the baseline, KPI definitions, dashboard, and cadence; budget impacts are signed off by finance.
Will you manage communications to staff and customers?
We prepare the plan, scripts, and assets and can deliver internal briefings. Customer communications are issued by your authorised owners.
