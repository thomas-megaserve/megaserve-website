Naturalisation & Citizenship Services

Practical guidance from residence accumulation through to citizenship—clear timelines, document packs, and structured liaison with authorities.

  • Eligibility mapping: continuous residence years, language, and good character
  • Route selection and plan (years of residence vs spouse-of-Cypriot)

  • Application forms, declarations, affidavits, and fee guidance

Process & Cadence

  1. Intake & Timeline Plan: confirm qualifying residence, travel gaps, and language readiness.
  2. Evidence Build: obtain police, language, civil status, and residence proofs; manage legalisations.
  3. Submission & Booking: lodge forms/fees; secure appointments; record reference numbers.
  4. Follow-ups: respond to authority queries; provide supplemental evidence as required.
  5. Decision & Passport Steps: collect approvals, oath/registration, and issue handover notes.

FAQ

How is eligibility for naturalisation assessed?
We review lawful residence years, travel gaps, language, and good character. A memo sets out the path and any remedial steps before filing.
Can family members apply with me?
Spouse-of-Cypriot and children may have separate or linked processes. We assess eligibility and prepare packs accordingly.
Do you handle translations and legalisations?
Yes—certified translations/apostilles are coordinated and documented in the evidence tracker.
How long do decisions take?
Timelines depend on office load and dossier completeness. We share current local estimates at intake and provide monthly status notes.
Will you advise on legal positions?
We manage administration, documentation, and liaison. Legal positions remain with your counsel; we coordinate closely to keep filings aligned.

Nothing here is legal or immigration advice; thresholds and positions are confirmed by your advisors and the competent authorities.
