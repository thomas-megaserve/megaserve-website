Project Management Services

Deliver regulatory and cross-functional initiatives on time with disciplined governance: chartered scope, RAID control, vendor management, and clear reporting to owners and boards.

What We Do

  • Project chartering: objectives, scope, success criteria, constraints.
  • Integrated plan & schedule: critical path, dependencies, buffers.
  • RAID governance: risks, assumptions, issues, decisions with owners.
  • Stakeholder cadence: stand-ups, workstream reviews, and SteerCo.
  • Vendor coordination: SOWs, deliverables, and acceptance criteria.
  • Regulatory/authority liaison tracking (where applicable).
  • Change control: CRs, impact analysis, and approval gates.
  • Cutover & post-implementation: rehearsals, Day-1 support, PIR.

Governance & Cadence Matrix

Layer Core Responsibilities Cadence / Outputs
Steering Committee Approve scope, budget, major changes; unblock escalations. Monthly / Decision log, gate approvals, headline KPIs.
PMO (MegaServe) Maintain plan, RAID, comms; coordinate vendors and owners. Weekly / Status pack, RAID updates, actions tracker.
Workstream Leads Own deliverables; manage resources; report variances. Weekly / Workstream minutes, burnup/burndown.
Vendors Deliver per SOW; surface risks; provide test evidence. Per milestone / Deliverable approvals, test reports.

We coordinate with your advisors and vendors; specialist engineering, legal, or tax advice remains with licensed providers.

Process

  1. Charter: Define objectives, scope, stakeholders, success metrics, and constraints. Establish RACI and comms channels.
  2. Plan: Build integrated schedule, dependencies, and resources. Baseline RAID and acceptance criteria.
  3. Execute: Drive workstreams, manage vendors, run stand-ups, and maintain status packs.
  4. Control: Change requests, variance analysis, risk responses, and decision logging.
  5. Cutover: Rehearsals, go/no-go gates, access & controls, and Day-1 support.
  6. Close: Post-Implementation Review (PIR), lessons learned, and benefits validation.

FAQ

This page is informational and not legal, tax, investment, or engineering advice. We coordinate with licensed professionals and vendors where required.

Do you replace internal project managers or consultants?
No. We operate the PMO and governance, integrate with your team, and coordinate vendors. Specialist design/build tasks remain with internal teams or appointed firms.
What tools do you use for scheduling and tracking?
We adapt to the client’s toolset and provide lightweight templates for plan, RAID, actions, and decision logs. We avoid tool sprawl and keep exports shareable.
Can you help with projects that involve regulators or banks?
Yes. We manage timelines, evidence lists, and liaison notes. Regulatory positions and submissions are owned by your licensed advisors; we coordinate the work.
How do you handle change requests without derailing timelines?
CRs are logged with impact on scope/cost/schedule and risk posture, reviewed by the CCB/SteerCo, and scheduled against available buffers and priorities.
Do you guarantee deadlines?
No. Delivery depends on third-party responsiveness and data quality. We set realistic gates, highlight risks early, and maintain clear decision logs.
