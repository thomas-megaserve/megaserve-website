Digital Nomad Visa — Cyprus

Move to Cyprus while working remotely for an overseas employer or your own company abroad. We confirm eligibility, prepare filings, and coordinate every step through approval and onboarding.

Overview

Cyprus’ Digital Nomad Visa provides a streamlined residency option for remote workers whose income is earned from outside Cyprus. MegaServe validates your route, curates your evidence pack, files your application, and keeps an audit-ready trail for renewals.

  • Eligibility scoping: remote-work status, income proofs, and insurance
  • Tailored document checklist with templates and translation guidance
  • End-to-end filing and authority liaison, including appointment booking
  • Family member add-ons (where eligible)
  • Onboarding coordination: Social Insurance (if applicable) and TIC/TIN
  • Renewal reminders and secure document repository

Process

  1. Discovery & route check — confirm digital nomad eligibility and timing.
  2. Checklist issue — gather income proofs, insurance, and police certificate; legalisation/translation where needed.
  3. Application build — forms, affidavits, and evidence pack.
  4. Submission & liaison — file, track, and coordinate appointments.
  5. Decision & collections — approvals, biometrics/permit issuance as applicable.
  6. Onboarding — TIC/TIN and practical setup (as relevant for your case).
  7. Renewals — reminders and document refresh schedule.

FAQ

Who qualifies for the Digital Nomad Visa in Cyprus?
Remote workers earning income from outside Cyprus with sufficient income, suitable accommodation, valid insurance, and clean records.
Can I work for Cypriot employers on this visa?
No. This route is for remote work with an employer or business established outside Cyprus.
How long does preparation and decision take?
Preparation typically takes 2–6 weeks depending on document readiness and legalisation. Decision times are set by authorities and vary by season and workload.
Can family members join me?
Yes, eligible dependants can be included on linked routes. We confirm eligibility at scoping and coordinate their filings.
Do my documents need legalisation or apostille?
Yes, most foreign-issued documents require legalisation/apostille and certified translation. We provide country-specific guidance.
