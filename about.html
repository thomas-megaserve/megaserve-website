About MegaServe

We combine corporate administration, compliance, and migration into one coordinated desk so your Cyprus footprint is clean, compliant, and easy to manage.

Overview

MegaServe supports growth companies, investors, and internationally mobile families establishing and operating in Cyprus. We orchestrate formations and governance, tax and VAT administration, banking coordination, nominee & fiduciary functions, and full-spectrum migration and residency services—built on controls you can audit.

  • One team across corporate, compliance, banking coordination, and mobility
  • Documented procedures and immutable evidence repositories
  • Transparent timelines with proactive reporting and query tracking
  • Templates aligned with current authority practice
  • Security-first handling: encryption in transit, data minimisation, least-privilege access

How We Work

  1. Scope & map — confirm objectives, regulators, dependencies, and critical dates.
  2. Evidence build — collect proofs via secure channels; create checklists and templates.
  3. Submission & liaison — file, track, and respond to authority queries with SLAs.
  4. Controls & reporting — maintain registers, archives, and status dashboards.
  5. Review & improve — quarterly control checks; update templates to current practice.
