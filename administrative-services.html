Administrative Services

Day-to-day corporate administration so your Cyprus entity stays organised, compliant, bankable, and responsive to counterparties and authorities.

Scope

  • Corporate record-keeping & minute books (digital + physical, where required)
  • Board & shareholder resolutions, PoAs, incumbency & good-standing docs
  • Registrar updates (officers, UBO/BoR, share capital), annual returns & renewals
  • Liaison with banks/EMIs, auditors, notaries, and authorities (Tax/VAT/Registrar)
  • Certifications, apostilles, legalised translations, couriering and document routing
  • Register management: directors, shareholders, charges, significant controllers
  • KYC refresh cycles, policy implementation, and light compliance housekeeping

Service Levels & Frequency

Activity Typical Frequency Target Response Notes
Routine corporate certificates / extracts As requested 1–3 business days Dependent on registrar queues and courier selection
Board / shareholder resolution drafting Ad hoc 1–2 business days Same-day available for straightforward templates
Registrar change filings Ad hoc 2–5 business days Processing time varies by change type and workload
KYC refresh / information requests Quarterly / Annual Same–2 business days Aligned to bank/auditor cycles to avoid repetition
Authority liaison (Tax/VAT/Registrar) Ad hoc Same–2 business days We provide status notes until matter is closed

Targets reflect typical handling; statutory or third-party turnaround can affect completion time.

Handover & Onboarding

  1. Intake: short call to map entities, signatories, banks, auditors, and current issues.
  2. Data Room: we create a secure folder structure and import key corporate records.
  3. Access: read-only access to banking/auditor portals where applicable (or proxy via PDFs).
  4. Calendar: we build a filings/renewals calendar (annual return, UBO/BoR, VAT/TIC, licenses).
  5. Templates: resolution/PoA templates aligned to your decision rights and governance style.
  6. Go-Live: confirm points of contact; begin handling daily requests under agreed targets.

Deliverables

  • Up-to-date corporate registers and minute books (verifiable trail)
  • Resolution and PoA template pack customised to your structure
  • Annual filings & renewals calendar with reminders
  • Authority correspondences archived and indexed
  • Banking dossier folder (KYC basics, activity narrative, signatory map)
  • Quarterly status note (open items, risks, upcoming renewals)

Authorities & Systems We Handle

  • Registrar of Companies (officer changes, annual return, BoR/UBO submissions)
  • Tax Department (TIC/TIN changes, basic communications)
  • VAT Service (registrations/updates coordination with your tax advisor)
  • Banks/EMIs (KYC refresh, signatory updates, reference letters, basic onboarding support)
  • Auditors & accountants (requests, confirmations, document packs)
  • Notaries / Ministry of Justice / MFA for apostilles and legalisations
  • Certified translations and couriering (tracked, with delivery proofs)

Documents Checklist (Onboarding)

  • Certificate set (Incorporation, Directors/Secretary, Shareholders, Registered Office)
  • Memorandum & Articles, current registers, minute book snapshots
  • IDs/PoAs for directors and UBOs; proof of address
  • Latest annual return and any recent registrar filings
  • Auditor details, bank details, and recent KYC requests (if any)

FAQ

Can you work alongside our existing accountants and auditors?
Yes. We coordinate documents and requests so your advisors receive what they need on time.
Do you provide certified copies and apostilles?
Yes. We arrange certifications and apostilles/legalisations and manage couriering end-to-end.
What if our records are incomplete?
We can reconstruct minute books and registers from registrar records and historical documents.
Can you manage multiple Cyprus entities?
Yes. We maintain separate calendars and registers, and provide consolidated status updates.
